# Supplement No. 2 to Part 743 Authorized Integrated Circuit Designer Know Your Customer (KYC) Vetting Form


This supplement contains a questionnaire that must be completed as part of the reporting requirements for authorized integrated circuit designers in ยง 743.9 of the EAR. The questions in the form are KYC best practices that are especially critical in this context where there is a risk of companies seeking advanced foundry services in circumvention of controls on advanced computing items. They are not an exhaustive list of due diligence requirements but provide important information that should be part of KYC screening.
