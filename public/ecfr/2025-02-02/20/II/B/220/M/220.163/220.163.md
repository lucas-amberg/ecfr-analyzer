# ยง 220.163   Employee penalty deductions.

If the employee earns over $400 in a month and does not report it within the time limit shown in ยง 220.162(b), a penalty is imposed. The penalty deduction for the first failure to report equals the annuity amount for the first month in which the employee earned over $400. The deduction for a second or later failure to report equals the annuity amount for each month in which the employee earned over $400 and failed to report it on time.
