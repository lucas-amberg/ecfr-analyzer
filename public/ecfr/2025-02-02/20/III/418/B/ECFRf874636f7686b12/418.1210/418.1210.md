# ยง 418.1210   What is not a major life-changing event?

We will not consider events other than those described in ยง 418.1205 to be major life-changing events. Certain types of events are not considered major life-changing events for the purposes of this subpart, such as:


(a) Events that affect your expenses, but not your income; or


(b) Events that result in the loss of dividend income because of the ordinary risk of investment.
