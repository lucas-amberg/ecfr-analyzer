# § 520.530   Cythioate oral liquid.

(a) *Specifications.* Each milliliter contains 15 milligrams of cythioate. 


(b) *Sponsor.* See Nos. 054771 and 058198 in § 510.600 of this chapter.


(c) *Conditions of use*—(1) *Amount.* 15 milligrams cythioate per 10 pounds of body weight every third day or twice a week.


(2) *Indications for use.* Dogs, for control of fleas.


(3) *Limitations.* Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.
