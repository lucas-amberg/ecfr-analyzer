# § 522.1008   Frunevetmab.

(a) *Specifications.* Each milliliter (mL) of solution contains 7 milligrams (mg) frunevetmab.


(b) *Sponsor.* See No. 054771 in § 510.600(c) of this chapter.


(c) *Conditions of use*—(1) *Cats*—(i) *Amount.* Administer once a month by subcutaneous injection the full contents of one or two 1-mL vials to achieve a minimum dosage of 0.45 mg/lb (1 mg/kg) body weight.


(ii) *Indications for use.* For the control of pain associated with osteoarthritis in cats.


(iii) *Limitations.* Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.
