# § 573.1020   Yellow prussiate of soda.

Yellow prussiate of soda (sodium ferrocyanide decahydrate: Na_4_Fe(Cn)_6_°-10H_2_O) may be safely used as an anticaking agent in salt for animal consumption at a level not to exceed 13 parts per million. The additive contains a minimum of 99.0 percent by weight of sodium ferrocyanide decahydrate.
