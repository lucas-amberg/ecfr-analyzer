# ยง 582.1480   Methylcellulose.

(a) *Product.* U.S.P. methylcellulose, except that the methoxy content shall not be less than 27.5 percent and not more than 31.5 percent on a dry-weight basis. 


(b) *Conditions of use.* This substance is generally recognized as safe when used in accordance with good manufacturing or feeding practice.
