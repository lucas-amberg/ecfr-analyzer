# ยง 607.21   Times for establishment registration and blood product listing.

If the owner or operator of the establishment has not previously entered into such operation (defined in ยง 607.3(d) of this chapter) for which a license is required, registration shall follow within 5 days after the submission of a biologics license application in order to manufacture blood products.
