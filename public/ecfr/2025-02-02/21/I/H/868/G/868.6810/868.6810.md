# ยง 868.6810   Tracheobronchial suction catheter.

(a) *Identification.* A tracheobronchial suction catheter is a device used to aspirate liquids or semisolids from a patient's upper airway.


(b) *Classification.* Class 1 (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 868.9.
