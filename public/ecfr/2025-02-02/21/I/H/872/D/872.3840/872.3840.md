# ยง 872.3840   Endodontic silver point.

(a) *Identification.* An endodontic silver point is a device made of silver intended for use during endodontic therapy to fill permanently the root canal of a tooth. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 872.9.
