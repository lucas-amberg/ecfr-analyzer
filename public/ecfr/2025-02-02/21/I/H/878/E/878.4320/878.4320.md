# ยง 878.4320   Removable skin clip.

(a) *Identification.* A removable skin clip is a clip-like device intended to connect skin tissues temporarily to aid healing. It is not absorbable. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 878.9.
