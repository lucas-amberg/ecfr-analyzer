# ยง 882.1560   Skin potential measurement device.

(a) *Identification.* A skin potential measurement device is a general diagnostic device used to measure skin voltage by means of surface skin electrodes. 


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 882.9.
