# ยง 882.4500   Cranioplasty material forming instrument.

(a) *Identification.* A cranioplasty material forming instrument is a roller used in the preparation and forming of cranioplasty (skull repair) materials. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 882.9.
