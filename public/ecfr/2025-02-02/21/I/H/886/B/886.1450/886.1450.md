# ยง 886.1450   Corneal radius measuring device.

(a) *Identification.* A corneal radius measuring device is an AC-powered device intended to measure corneal size by superimposing the image of the cornea on a scale at the focal length of the lens of a small, hand held, single tube penscope or eye gauge magnifier.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 886.9, only when the device does not include computer software in the unit or topographers.
