# ยง 886.4750   Ophthalmic eye shield.

(a) *Identification.* An ophthalmic eye shield is a device that consists of a plastic or aluminum eye covering intended to protect the eye or retain dressing materials in place. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). When made only of plastic or aluminum, the device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 886.9.
