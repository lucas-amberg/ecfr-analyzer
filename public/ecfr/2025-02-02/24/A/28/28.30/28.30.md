# ยง 28.30   Response.

(a) The respondent may file a written response to the complaint, in accordance with ยง 26.30 of this title, within 30 days of service of the complaint. The response shall be deemed to be a request for a hearing. The response must include the admission or denial of each allegation of liability made in the complaint; any defense on which the respondent intends to rely; any reasons why the penalties and assessments should be less than the amount set forth in the complaint; and the name, address, and telephone number of the person who will act as the respondent's representative, if any.


(b) *Failure to respond.* If no response is submitted, HUD may file a motion for default judgment in accordance with ยง 26.41 of this chapter.
