# ยง 170.804   Who should be contacted if a Tribal transportation facility is not being maintained to TTP standards due to insufficient funding?

The Tribe may notify BIA or FHWA if the Tribe believes that a facility on the NTTFI is not being adequately maintained to the standards identified in ยง 170.803. If BIA or FHWA determines that a Tribal transportation facility is not being maintained, it will:


(a) Notify the facility owner;


(b) Provide a draft copy of the report to the affected Tribe for comment before forwarding it to Secretary of Transportation; and


(c) Report these findings to the appropriate office within FHWA.
