# ยง 293.14   When does a compact or amendment take effect?

(a) A compact or amendment, that is affirmatively approved or approved by operation of law, takes effect on the date that notice of its approval is published in the _Federal Register_.


(b) The notice of affirmative approval or approval by operation of law must be published in the _Federal Register_ within 90 days from the date the compact or amendment is received by the Office of Indian Gaming.
