# ยง 700.81   Monthly housing cost.

(a) *General.* The term monthly housing cost for a replacement dwelling purchased by a certified eligible head of household is the average monthly cost for all mortgage payments, real property taxes, reasonable utility charges, and insurance.


(b) *Computation of monthly housing cost for replacement dwelling.* A person's monthly housing cost for a replacement dwelling shall be a projected amount that includes one-twelfth of the estimated reasonable annual cost for utility charges.
