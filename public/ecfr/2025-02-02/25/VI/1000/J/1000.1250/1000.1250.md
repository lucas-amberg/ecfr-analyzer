If the Secretary fails to make a determination with respect to a waiver request within the period specified in ยง 1000.1240 (including any extension agreed to under that section), the waiver request is automatically, by operation of law,


(a) Deemed approved except for programs eligible under section 403(b)(2) or section 403(c) (25 U.S.C. 5363(b)(2) or 5363(c)), as amended; or
