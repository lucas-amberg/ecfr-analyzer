# § 51.759   U.S. No. 3.

“U.S. No. 3” consists of grapefruit which meet the following requirements:


(a) Basic requirements:


(1) Mature;


(2) Misshapen;


(3) Poorly colored;


(4) Rough texture, not seriously bumpy;


(5) Similar varietal characteristics; and,


(6) Slightly spongy.


(b) Free from:


(1) Decay;


(2) Unhealed skin breaks; and,


(3) Wormy fruit.


(c) Free from very serious damage caused by:


(1) Ammoniation;


(2) Bruises;


(3) Buckskin;


(4) Caked melanose;


(5) Disease;


(6) Dryness or mushy condition;


(7) Hail;


(8) Insects;


(9) Oil spotting;


(10) Scab;


(11) Scale;


(12) Scars;


(13) Skin breakdown;


(14) Sprayburn;


(15) Sprouting;


(16) Sunburn; and,


(17) Other means.


(d) For tolerances see § 51.760.
