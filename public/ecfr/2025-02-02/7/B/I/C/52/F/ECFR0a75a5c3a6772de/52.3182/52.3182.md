# ยง 52.3182   Varietal types of dried prunes.

(a) *Type I.* French; or Robe; or a mixture of French and Robe.


(b) *Type II.* Italian.


(c) *Type III.* Imperial; or Sugar; or a mixture of Imperial and Sugar.


(d) *Type IV.* Any other types; or mixtures of any types other than mixtures in Type I and Type III of this section.
