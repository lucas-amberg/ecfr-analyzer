# ยง 65.135   Covered commodity.

(a) *Covered commodity* means:


(1) Muscle cuts of lamb, chicken, and goat;


(2) Ground lamb, ground chicken, and ground goat;


(3) Perishable agricultural commodities;


(4) Peanuts;


(5) Macadamia nuts;


(6) Pecans; and


(7) Ginseng.


(b) Covered commodities are excluded from this part if the commodity is an ingredient in a processed food item as defined in ยง 65.220.
