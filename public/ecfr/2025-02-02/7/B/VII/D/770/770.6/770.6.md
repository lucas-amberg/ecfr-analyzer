# ยง 770.6   Rates and terms.

(a) *Term.* Each loan will be scheduled for repayment over a period not to exceed 40 years from the date of the note. 


(b) *Interest rate.* The interest rate charged by the Agency will be the lower of the interest rate in effect at the time of the loan approval or loan closing, which is the current rate available in any FSA office. The rate will be equal to the interest rate for direct farm ownership loans not to exceed 5 percent. Except as provided in ยง 770.10(b) of this chapter, the interest rate will be fixed for the life of the loan.
