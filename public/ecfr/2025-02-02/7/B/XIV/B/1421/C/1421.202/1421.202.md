# ยง 1421.202   Loan deficiency payment quantity.

(a) A LDP may be based on 100 percent of the net eligible quantity specified on acceptable evidence of production of the commodity certified as eligible for LDP if such production evidence is provided for such commodity under ยง 1421.12. 


(b) Two or more producers may obtain a single joint LDP for commodities that are stored in the same storage facility. Two or more producers may obtain individual LDPs for their share of the commodity that is stored commingled in a farm storage facility with commodities for which an LDP has been requested and will be liable for any damage incurred by CCC for incorrect certification of such commodities under ยง 1421.203.


(c) Two or more producers may obtain a single joint LDP for commodities that are stored in an authorized or unauthorized warehouse if the acceptable documentation representing an eligible commodity for which a LDP is requested is completed jointly for such producers.
