# § 4290.300   When and how to apply for a RBIC License.

(a) *Notice of Funds Availability (“NOFA”).* The Agency will publish a NOFA in the _Federal Register_ advising potential applicants of the availability of funds for the RBIC program and inviting the submission of applications. The NOFA may specify limitations, special rules, procedures, and restrictions for a particular funding round. When submitting its application, an Applicant must comply with both this part 4290 and any requirements specified in the NOFA, including the opening and closing dates for submission of an application.


(b) *Application form.* An Applicant must apply for a RBIC license using an appropriate application packet provided by the Agency. Upon receipt of a completed application packet, the Agency may request clarifying or technical information on the materials submitted as part of the application.
