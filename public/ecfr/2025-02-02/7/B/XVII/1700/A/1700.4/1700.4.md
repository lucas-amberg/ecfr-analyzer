# ยง 1700.4   Public comments on proposed rules.

RUS requires that all persons submitting comments to a proposed rule or other document published by the agency in the _Federal Register,_ submit comments as specified in the published notice. Copies of comments submitted are available to the public in conformance with 7 CFR part 1.
