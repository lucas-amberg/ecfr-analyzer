Closing is the process by which Rural Development determines that applicable administrative actions have been completed and the Grant Agreement is signed. The Grant Agreement (Exhibit A) will be executed by the State Director at the time the Form RD 1940-1 and Grant Agreement is sent to the Grantee in accordance with ยง 1944.533 (f)(4).
