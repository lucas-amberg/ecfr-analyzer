# ยง 101.7   Seed organisms.

When used in conjunction with or in reference to seed organisms, the following shall mean:


(a) *Master Seed.* An organism at a specific passage level which has been selected and permanently stored by the producer from which all other seed passages are derived within permitted levels.


(b) *Working Seed.* An organism at a passage level between Master Seed and Production Seed.


(c) *Production Seed.* An organism at a specified passage level which is used without further propagation for initiating preparation of a fraction.
