# ยง 146.21   Definitions.

*Table-egg layer.* A domesticated chicken grown for the primary purpose of producing eggs for human consumption.


*Table-egg layer pullet.* A sexually immature domesticated chicken grown for the primary purpose of producing eggs for human consumption.
