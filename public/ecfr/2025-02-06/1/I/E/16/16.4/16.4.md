# ยง 16.4   Authorizing duties.

The agency authorizing officer is responsible for furnishing, to the Director of the Federal Register, a current mailing list of officers or employees of the agency who are authorized to receive the _Federal Register,_ the Code of Federal Regulations, and the Weekly Compilation of Presidential Documents for official use.
