# ยง 501.65   Publication of notice of availability of draft EIS.

A Notice of Availability of any draft EIS will be published in the _Federal Register_ and comments thereon will also be solicited. Interested persons may request a hearing on any draft EIS. Such hearing must be requested within thirty (30) days of publication of the Notice of Availability of the draft EIS.
