# ยง 708.33   Procedure for appeals.

(a) By the 15th day after filing a notice of appeal under ยง 708.32, the appellant must file a statement identifying the issues that it wishes the OHA Director to review. A copy of the statement must be served on the other parties, who may file a response by the 20th day after receipt of the statement. Any response must also be served on the other parties.


(b) In considering the appeal, the OHA Director:


(1) Will possess all powers necessary to adjudicate the appeal.


(2) Will review findings of fact for clear error and conclusions of law *de novo;* and


(3) Will close the record on appeal after receiving the last submission permitted under this section.
