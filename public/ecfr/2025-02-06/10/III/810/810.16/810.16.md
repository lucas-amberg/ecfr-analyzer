# ยง 810.16   Effective date and savings clause.

(a) The regulations in this part are effective March 25, 2015.


(b) Except for actions that may be taken by DOE pursuant to ยง 810.10, the regulations in this part do not affect the validity or terms of any specific authorizations granted under regulations in effect before March 25, 2015 or generally authorized activities under those regulations for which the contracts, purchase orders, or licensing arrangements were already in effect.
