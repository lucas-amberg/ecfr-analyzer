# ยง 112.2   Public availability of requests (52 U.S.C. 30108(d)).

(a) Advisory opinion requests which qualify under 11 CFR 112.1 shall be made public at the Commission promptly upon their receipt.


(b) A copy of the original request and any supplements thereto, shall be available for public inspection at the Public Disclosure and Media Relations Division of the Commission and on the Commission's website
