# ยง 16.30   Request for interpretive advice or no-objection letter.

Any person requesting interpretive advice or a no-objection letter from the OCC with respect to any provision of this part shall: 


(a) File a copy of the request, including any supporting attachments, with the OCC's Law Department at the address provided at *www.occ.gov*;


(b) Identify or describe the provisions of this part to which the request relates, the participants in the proposed transaction, and the reasons for the request; and 


(c) Include with the request a legal opinion as to each legal issue raised and an accounting opinion as to each accounting issue raised.
