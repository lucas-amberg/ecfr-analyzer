(a) *Authority.* 12 U.S.C. 56, 60, and 93a. 


(b) *Scope.* Except as otherwise provided, the restrictions in this subpart apply to the declaration and payment of all dividends by a national bank, including dividends paid in property. However, the provisions contained in ยง 5.64 do not apply to dividends paid in stock of the bank. 


(c) *Exceptions to the rules of general applicability.* Sections 5.8, 5.10, and 5.11 do not apply to this subpart.
