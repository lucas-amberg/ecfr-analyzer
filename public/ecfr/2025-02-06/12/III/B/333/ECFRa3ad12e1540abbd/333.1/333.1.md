# ยง 333.1   Classification of general character of business.

State nonmember insured banks are divided into five categories for the purpose of classifying their general character or type of business, 
2 viz: commercial banks, banks and trust companies, savings banks (including mutual and stock), industrial banks, and cash depositories.
