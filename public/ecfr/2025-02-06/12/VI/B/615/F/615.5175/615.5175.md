# ยง 615.5175   Investments in Farm Credit System institution preferred stock.

Except as provided for in ยง 615.5171, Farm Credit banks, associations and service corporations may only purchase preferred stock issued by another Farm Credit System institution, including the Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation, with the written prior approval of the Farm Credit Administration.
