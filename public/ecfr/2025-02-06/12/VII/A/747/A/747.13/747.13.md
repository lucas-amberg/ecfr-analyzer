Except as otherwise provided by law, the ALJ may, for good cause shown, extend the time limits prescribed by the Uniform Rules or by any notice or order issued in the proceedings. After the referral of the case to the NCUA Board pursuant to ยง 747.38, the NCUA Board may grant extensions of the time limits for good cause shown.
