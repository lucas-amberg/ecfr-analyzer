# ยง 301.7   Investment Assistance application.

(a) For all EDA Investment Assistance programs, including the Public Works, Economic Adjustment Assistance, Planning, Local Technical Assistance, Research and National Technical Assistance, and University Center programs, EDA will publish an FFO that specifies application submission requirements and evaluation procedures and criteria. Each FFO will be published on the EDA Web site and at *http://www.grants.gov*. All forms required for EDA Investment Assistance may be obtained electronically from *http://www.grants.gov* or from the appropriate regional office.


(b) PWEDA does not require nor does EDA provide an appeals process for denial of applications or EDA Investment Assistance.
