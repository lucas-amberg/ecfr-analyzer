# ยง 303.2   Definitions.

In addition to the defined terms set forth in ยง 300.3 of this chapter, the following terms used in this part shall have the following meanings:


*Planning Investment* means the award of EDA Investment Assistance under section 203 of PWEDA and this part.


*Planning Organization* means a Recipient whose purpose is to develop and implement a CEDS for a specific EDA-approved Region under section 203 of PWEDA.


*Strategy Committee* means the committee or other entity identified by the Planning Organization as responsible for the development, implementation, revision or replacement of the CEDS for the Planning Organization.
