# ยง 399.73   Definition of small business for Regulatory Flexibility Act.

For the purposes of the Department's implementation of chapter 6 of title 5, United States Code (Regulatory Flexibility Act), a direct air carrier or foreign air carrier is a small business if it provides air transportation only with small aircraft as defined in ยง 298.2 of this chapter (up to 60 seats/18,000 pound payload capacity).
