# ยง 431.39   Mission rules, procedures, contingency plans, and checklists.

(a) An applicant shall submit mission rules, procedures, checklists, emergency plans, and contingency abort plans, if any, that ensure safe conduct of mission operations during nominal and non-nominal vehicle flight. 


(b) Mission rules, procedures, checklists, emergency plans, and contingency abort plans must be contained in a safety directive, notebook, or other compilation that is approved by the safety official designated under ยง 431.33(c) and concurred in by the launch site operator and reentry site operator, if any. 


(c) Vehicle safety operations personnel must have current and consistent mission checklists.
