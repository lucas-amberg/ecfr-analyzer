# ยง 295.7   Notice of availability of funds.

The Program shall publish at least annually a _Federal Register_ notice inviting interested parties to submit proposals, and may more frequently publish invitations for proposals in the Commerce Business Daily, based upon the annual notice. Proposals must be submitted in accordance with the guidelines in the ATP Proposal Preparation Kit as identified in the published notice. Proposals will only be considered for funding when submitted in response to an invitation published in the _Federal Register,_ or a related announcement in the Commerce Business Daily.
