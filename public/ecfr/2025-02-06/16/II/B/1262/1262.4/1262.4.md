# ยง 1262.4   Test procedure for determining flux index.

(a) Select at least one loose or separable magnet of each shape and size in the *subject magnet product.*

(b) Measure the flux index of each selected magnet in accordance with the procedure in section 8.25.1 through 8.25.3 of ASTM F963-17, *Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Toy Safety,* approved on May 1, 2017.
