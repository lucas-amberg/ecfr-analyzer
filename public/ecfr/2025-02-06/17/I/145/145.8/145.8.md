# ยง 145.8   Fees for records services.

A schedule of fees for record services, including locating, and making records available, and copying, appears in appendix B to this part. Copies of the schedule of fees may also be obtained upon request made in person, by telephone or by mail from the FOIA compliance staff or at any regional office of the Commission.
