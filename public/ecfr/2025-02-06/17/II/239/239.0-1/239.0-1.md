# ยง 239.0-1   Availability of forms.

(a) This part identifies and describes the forms prescribed for use under the Securities Act of 1933.


(b) Any person may obtain a copy of any form prescribed for use in this part by written request to the Securities and Exchange Commission, 100 F Street, NE, Washington, DC 20549. Any persons may inspect the forms at this address and at the Commission's regional offices. (*See* ยง 200.11 of this chapter for the addresses of the SEC regional offices.)
