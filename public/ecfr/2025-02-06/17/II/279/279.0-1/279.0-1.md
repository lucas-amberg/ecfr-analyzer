# ยง 279.0-1   Availability of forms.

(a) This part identifies and describes the forms prescribed for use under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940.


(b) Any person may obtain a copy of any form prescribed for use in this part by written request to the Securities and Exchange Commission, 100 F Street, NE., Washington, DC 20549. Any person may inspect the forms at this address and at the Commission's regional offices. (See ยง 200.11 of this chapter for the addresses of SEC regional offices.)
