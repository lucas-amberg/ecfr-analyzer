# ยง 385.1504   Maximum civil penalty (Rule 1504).

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, the Commission may assess a civil penalty of up to $28,618 for each day that the violation continues.


(b) No civil penalty may be assessed where a license or exemption is ordered revoked.
