# ยง 177.30   Review of final determinations.

Any party-at-interest listed in ยง 177.22(d) may seek judicial review of a final determination within 30 days after publication of such determination in the _Federal Register,_ and may seek judicial review of a refusal to issue a final determination within 30 days after such refusal. The Court of International Trade shall have exclusive jurisdiction to review a final determination or a refusal to issue a final determination made under this subpart.
