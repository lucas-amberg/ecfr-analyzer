# ยง 1120.3   DoD Component implementation.

Each DoD Component that awards grants or cooperative agreements must:


(a) Conform the format of its awards to the standard format established by this part no later than [18 months after the effective date of the final rule].


(b) Update electronic systems it maintains for generating awards within 18 months of the issuance of a new or updated DoD form for the award cover pages, in order to implement that form in those systems, unless it has an approved deviation in accordance with ยง 1120.110.
