# ยง 402.165   Publications for sale through the Government Publishing Office.

The public may purchase publications containing information pertaining to the program, organization, functions, and procedures of SSA from the electronic U.S. Government Bookstore maintained by the Government Publishing Office. The publications for sale include but are not limited to:


(a) Title 20, parts 400 through 499, of the Code of Federal Regulations;


(b) _Federal Register_ issues; and


(c) Compilation of the Social Security Laws.
