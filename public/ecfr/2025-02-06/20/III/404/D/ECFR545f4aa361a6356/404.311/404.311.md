# ยง 404.311   When does my entitlement to old-age benefits begin and end?

(a) We will find you entitled to old-age benefits beginning with: 


(1) If you have attained full retirement age (as defined in ยง 404.409), the first month covered by your application *in* which you meet all requirements for entitlement; or
