# ยง 653.102   Job information.

All SWAs must make job order information conspicuous and available to MSFWs by all reasonable means. Such information must, at minimum, be available through internet labor exchange systems and through the one-stop centers. SWAs must ensure ES staff at one-stop centers provide assistance to MSFWs to access job order information easily and efficiently.
