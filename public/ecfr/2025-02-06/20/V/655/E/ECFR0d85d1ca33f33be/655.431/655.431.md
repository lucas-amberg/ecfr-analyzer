# ยง 655.431   Notice of Deficiency.

(a) *Notification.* If the CO determines the *CW-1 Application for Temporary Employment Certification* contains errors or inaccuracies, or does not meet the requirements set forth in this subpart, the CO will issue a NOD to the employer and, if applicable, the employer's attorney or agent.


(b) *Notice content.* The NOD will:


(1) State the reason(s) the *CW-1 Application for Temporary Employment Certification* fails to meet the criteria for acceptance;


(2) Offer the employer an opportunity to submit a modified *CW-1 Application for Temporary Employment Certification* within 10 business days from the date of the NOD, and state the modification that is required for the CO to issue a NOA; and


(3) State that if the employer does not comply with the requirements of ยง 655.432 for submitting a modified application, the CO will deny the *CW-1 Application for Temporary Employment Certification.*
