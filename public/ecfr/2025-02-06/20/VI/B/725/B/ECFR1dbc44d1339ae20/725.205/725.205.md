# § 725.205   Determination of dependency; spouse.

For the purposes of augmenting benefits, an individual who is the miner's spouse (see § 725.204) will be determined to be dependent upon the miner if:


(a) The individual is a member of the same household as the miner (see § 725.232); or


(b) The individual is receiving regular contributions from the miner for support (see § 725.233(c)); or


(c) The miner has been ordered by a court to contribute to such individual's support (see § 725.233(e)); or


(d) The individual is the natural parent of the son or daughter of the miner; or


(e) The individual was married to the miner (see § 725.204) for a period of not less than 1 year.
