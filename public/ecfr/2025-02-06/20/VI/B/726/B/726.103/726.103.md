# ยง 726.103   Application for authority to self-insure; effect of regulations contained in this part.

As appropriate, each of the regulations, interpretations, and requirements contained in this part, including those described in subpart C of this part, are binding upon each applicant under this subpart, and the applicant's consent to be bound by all requirements of the regulations in this part are deemed to be included in and a part of the application, as fully as though written therein.
