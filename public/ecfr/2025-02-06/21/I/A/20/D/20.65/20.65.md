# ยง 20.65   National defense and foreign policy.

(a) Records or information may be withheld from public disclosure if they are:


(1) Specifically authorized under criteria established by an Executive order to be kept secret in the interest of national defense or foreign policy; and


(2) In fact properly classified under such Executive order.
