# ยง 117.7   Applicability of subparts C, D, and G of this part to a facility solely engaged in the storage of unexposed packaged food.

(a) *Applicability of subparts C and G.* Subparts C and G of this part do not apply to a facility solely engaged in the storage of unexposed packaged food.


(b) *Applicability of subpart D.* A facility solely engaged in the storage of unexposed packaged food, including unexposed packaged food that requires time/temperature control to significantly minimize or prevent the growth of, or toxin production by, pathogens is subject to the modified requirements in ยง 117.206 for any unexposed packaged food that requires time/temperature control to significantly minimize or prevent the growth of, or toxin production by, pathogens.
