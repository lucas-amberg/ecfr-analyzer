# ยง 117.8   Applicability of subpart B of this part to the off-farm packing and holding of raw agricultural commodities.

Except as provided by ยง 117.5(k)(1), subpart B of this part applies to the off-farm packaging, packing, and holding of raw agricultural commodities.
