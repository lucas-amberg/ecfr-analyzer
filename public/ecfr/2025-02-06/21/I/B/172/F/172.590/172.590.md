# ยง 172.590   Yeast-malt sprout extract.

Yeast-malt sprout extract, as described in this section, may be safely used in food in accordance with the following prescribed conditions: 


(a) The additive is produced by partial hydrolysis of yeast extract (derived from *Saccharomyces cereviseae, Saccharomyces fragilis,* or *Candida utilis*) using the sprout portion of malt barley as the source of enzymes. The additive contains a maximum of 6 percent 5โฒ nucleotides by weight.


(b) The additive may be used as a flavor enhancer in food at a level not in excess of that reasonably required to produce the intended effect.
