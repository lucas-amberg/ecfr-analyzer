# ยง 182.3637   Potassium metabisulfite.

(a) *Product.* Potassium metabisulfite.


(b) [Reserved]


(c) *Limitations, restrictions, or explanation.* This substance is generally recognized as safe when used in accordance with good manufacturing practice, except that it is not used in meats; in food recognized as a source of vitamin B_1_; on fruits and vegetables intended to be served raw to consumers or sold raw to consumers, or to be presented to consumers as fresh.
