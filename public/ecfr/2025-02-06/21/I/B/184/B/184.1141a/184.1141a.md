# ยง 184.1141a   Ammonium phosphate, monobasic.

(a) Ammonium phosphate, monobasic (NH_4_H_2_PO_4_, CAS Reg. No. 7722-76-1) is manufactured by reacting ammonia with phosphoric acid at a pH below 5.8.


(b) The ingredient meets the specifications of the Food Chemicals Codex, 3d Ed. (1981), p. 21, which is incorporated by reference. Copies are available from the National Academy Press, 2101 Constitution Ave. NW., Washington, DC 20418, or available for inspection at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA). For information on the availability of this material at NARA, call 202-741-6030, or go to: *http://www.archives.gov/federal_register/code_of_federal_regulations/ibr_locations.html.*

The affirmation of this ingredient as generally recognized as safe (GRAS) as a direct human food ingredient is based upon the following current good manufacturing practice conditions of use:


(1) The ingredient is used as a dough strengthener as defined in ยง 170.3(o)(6) of this chapter and a pH control agent as defined in ยง 170.3(o)(23) of this chapter.

(d) Prior sanctions for this ingredient different from the uses established in this section do not exist or have been waived.
