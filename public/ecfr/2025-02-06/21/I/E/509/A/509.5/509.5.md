# ยง 509.5   Petitions.

The Commissioner of Food and Drugs, either on his own initiative or on behalf of any interested person who has submitted a petition, may issue a proposal to establish, revoke, or amend a regulation under this part. Any such petition shall include an adequate factual basis to support the petition, shall be in the form set forth in ยง 10.30 of this chapter, and will be published in the _Federal Register_ for comment if it contains reasonable grounds for the proposed regulation.
