# § 520.2150   Stanozolol.

(a) *Specifications.* Each tablet or chewable tablet contains 2 milligrams stanozolol.


(b) *Sponsor.* No. 054771 in § 510.600(c) of this chapter.


(c) *Conditions of use in dogs and cats*—(1) *Amount*—(i) *Dogs:* Administered orally to small breeds, 
1/2 to 1 tablet twice daily for several weeks; to large breeds, 1 to 2 tablets twice daily for several weeks. The tablets may be crushed and administered in feed.


(ii) *Cats:* Administered orally 
1/2 to 1 tablet twice daily for several weeks.


(2) *Indications for use.* As an anabolic steroid treatment.


(3) *Limitations.* Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.
