# § 556.344   Ivermectin.

(a) *Acceptable daily intake (ADI).* The ADI for total residue of ivermectin is 5 µg/kg of body weight per day.


(b) *Tolerances.* The tolerances for 22,23-dihydroavermectin B_1_a (marker residue) are:


(1) *American bison.* Liver (target tissue): 15 ppb.


(2) *Cattle.* (i) Liver (target tissue): 1.6 ppm.


(ii) Muscle: 650 ppb.


(3) *Reindeer.* Liver (target tissue): 15 ppb.


(4) *Sheep.* Liver (target tissue): 30 ppb.


(5) *Swine.* (i) Liver (target tissue): 20 ppb.


(ii) Muscle: 20 ppb.


(c) *Related conditions of use.* See §§ 520.1192, 520.1195, 520.1197, 522.1192, 522.1193, 524.1193, and 558.300 of this chapter.
