# § 556.68   Azaperone.

(a) *Acceptable daily intake (ADI).* The ADI for total residue of azaperone is 0.63 µg/kg of body weight per day.


(b) *Tolerances.* The tolerance for azaperone is:


(1) *Swine.* Edible tissues: Not required.
