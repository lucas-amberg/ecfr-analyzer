# § 582.1745   Sodium carboxymethylcellulose.

(a) *Product.* Sodium carboxymethyl- cellulose is the sodium salt of carboxymethylcellulose not less than 99.5 percent on a dry-weight basis, with maximum substitution of 0.95 carboxymethyl groups per anhydroglucose unit, and with a minimum viscosity of 25 centipoises for 2 percent by weight aqueous solution at 25 °C. 


(b) *Conditions of use.* This substance is generally recognized as safe when used in accordance with good manufacturing or feeding practice.
