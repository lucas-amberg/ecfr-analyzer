# ยง 582.2227   Calcium silicate.

(a) *Product.* Calcium silicate. 


(b) *Tolerance.* 2 percent and 5 percent. 


(c) *Limitations, restrictions, or explanation.* This substance is generally recognized as safe when used at levels not exceeding 2 percent in table salt and 5 percent in baking powder in accordance with good manufacturing or feeding practice.
