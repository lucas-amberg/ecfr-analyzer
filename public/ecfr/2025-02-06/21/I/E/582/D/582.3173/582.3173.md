# ยง 582.3173   Butylated hydroxytoluene.

(a) *Product.* Butylated hydroxytol- uene. 


(b) *Tolerance.* This substance is generally recognized as safe for use in food when the total content of antioxidants is not over 0.02 percent of fat or oil content, including essential (volatile) oil content of food provided the substance is used in accordance with good manufacturing or feeding practice.
