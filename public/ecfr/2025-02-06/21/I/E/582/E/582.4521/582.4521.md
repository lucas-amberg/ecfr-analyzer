# ยง 582.4521   Monosodium phosphate derivatives of mono- and diglycerides of edible fats or oils, or edible fat-forming fatty acids.

(a) *Product.* Monosodium phosphate derivatives of mono- and diglycerides of edible fats or oils, or edible fat-forming fatty acids. 


(b) *Conditions of use.* This substance is generally recognized as safe when used in accordance with good manufacturing or feeding practice.
