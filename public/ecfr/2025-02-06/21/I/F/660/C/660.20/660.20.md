# ยง 660.20   Blood Grouping Reagent.

(a) *Proper name and definition.* The proper name of this product shall be Blood Grouping Reagent and it shall consist of an antibody-containing fluid containing one or more of the blood grouping antibodies listed in ยง 660.28(a)(4).


(b) *Source.* The source of this product shall be blood, plasma, serum, or protein-rich fluids, such as those derived from stable immunoglobulin-secreting cell lines maintained either in tissue cultures or in secondary hosts.
