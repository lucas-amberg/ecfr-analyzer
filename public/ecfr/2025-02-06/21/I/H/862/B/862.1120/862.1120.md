# ยง 862.1120   Blood gases (P

(a) *Identification.* A blood gases (P_CO2_, P_O2_) and blood pH test system is a device intended to measure certain gases in blood, serum, plasma or pH of blood, serum, and plasma. Measurements of blood gases (P_CO2_, P_O2_) and blood pH are used in the diagnosis and treatment of life-threatening acid-base disturbances.


(b) *Classification.* Class II.
