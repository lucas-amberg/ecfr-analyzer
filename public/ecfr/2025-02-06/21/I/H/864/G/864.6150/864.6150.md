# ยง 864.6150   Capillary blood collection tube.

(a) *Identification.* A capillary blood collection tube is a plain or heparinized glass tube of very small diameter used to collect blood by capillary action. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 864.9.
