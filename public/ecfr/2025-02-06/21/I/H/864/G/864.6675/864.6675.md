# ยง 864.6675   Platelet aggregometer.

(a) *Identification.* A platelet aggregometer is a device, used to determine changes in platelet shape and platelet aggregation following the addition of an aggregating reagent to a platelet rich plasma. 


(b) *Classification.* Class II (performance standards).
