# ยง 864.9125   Vacuum-assisted blood collection system.

(a) *Identification.* A vacuum-assisted blood collection system is a device intended for medical purposes that uses a vacuum to draw blood for subsequent reinfusion. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The manual device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 864.9.
