# ยง 864.9275   Blood bank centrifuge for in vitro diagnostic use.

(a) *Identification.* A blood bank centrifuge for in vitro diagnostic use is a device used only to separate blood cells for further diagnostic testing. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 864.9.
