# ยง 864.9575   Environmental chamber for storage of platelet concentrate.

(a) *Identification.* An environmental chamber for storage of platelet concentrate is a device used to hold platelet-rich plasma within a preselected temperature range.


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 864.9.
