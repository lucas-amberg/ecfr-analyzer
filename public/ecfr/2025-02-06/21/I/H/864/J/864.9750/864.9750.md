# ยง 864.9750   Heat-sealing device.

(a) *Identification.* A heat-sealing device is a device intended for medical purposes that uses heat to seal plastic bags containing blood or blood components. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 864.9.
