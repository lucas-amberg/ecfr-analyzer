# ยง 866.5170   Breast milk immunological test system.

(a) *Identification.* A breast milk immunological test system is a device that consists of the reagents used to measure by immunochemical techniques the breast milk proteins.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 866.9.
