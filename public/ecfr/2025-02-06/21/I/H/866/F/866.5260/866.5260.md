# ยง 866.5260   Complement C

(a) *Identification.* A complement C_3b_ inactivator immunological test system is a device that consists of the reagents used to measure by immunochemical techniques the complement C_3b_ inactivator (a plasma protein) in serum. Complement is a group of serum proteins that destroy infectious agents. Measurement of complement C_3b_ inactivator aids in the diagnosis of inherited antibody dysfunction.


(b) *Classification.* Class II (performance standards).
