# ยง 868.6400   Calibration gas.

(a) *Identification.* A calibration gas is a device consisting of a container of gas of known concentration intended to calibrate medical gas concentration measurement devices.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 868.9.
