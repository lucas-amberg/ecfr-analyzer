# ยง 870.4430   Cardiopulmonary bypass intracardiac suction control.

(a) *Identification.* A cardiopulmonary bypass intracardiac suction control is a device which provides the vacuum and control for a cardiotomy return sucker. 


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 870.9.
