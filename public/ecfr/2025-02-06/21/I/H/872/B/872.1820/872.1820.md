# ยง 872.1820   Dental x-ray exposure alignment device.

(a) *Identification.* A dental x-ray exposure alignment device is a device intended to position x-ray film and to align the examination site with the x-ray beam.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 872.9.
