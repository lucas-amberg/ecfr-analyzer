# ยง 876.4500   Mechanical lithotriptor.

(a) *Identification.* A mechanical lithotriptor is a device with steel jaws that is inserted into the urinary bladder through the urethra to grasp and crush bladder stones. 


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device, when it is a biliary mechanical lithotripter, is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 876.9.
