# ยง 878.4700   Surgical microscope and accessories.

(a) *Identification.* A surgical microscope and accessories is an AC-powered device intended for use during surgery to provide a magnified view of the surgical field.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 878.9.
