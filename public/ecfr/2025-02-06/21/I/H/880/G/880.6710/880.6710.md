# ยง 880.6710   Medical ultraviolet water purifier.

(a) *Identification.* A medical ultraviolet water purifier is a device intended for medical purposes that is used to destroy bacteria in water by exposure to ultraviolet radiation.


(b) *Classification.* Class II (performance standards). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in part 807, subpart E, of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 880.9.
