# ยง 884.3200   Cervical drain.

(a) *Identification.* A cervical drain is a device designed to provide an exit channel for draining discharge from the cervix after pelvic surgery. 


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 884.9.
