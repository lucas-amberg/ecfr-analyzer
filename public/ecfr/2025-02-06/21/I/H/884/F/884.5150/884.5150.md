# ยง 884.5150   Nonpowered breast pump.

(a) *Identification.* A nonpowered breast pump is a manual suction device used to express milk from the breast. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I. The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 884.9, if the device is using either a bulb or telescoping mechanism which does not develop more than 250 mm Hg suction, and the device materials that contact breast or breast milk do not produce cytotoxicity, irritation, or sensitization effects.
