# ยง 884.5920   Vaginal insufflator.

(a) *Identification.* A vaginal insufflator is a device used to treat vaginitis by introducing medicated powder from a hand-held bulb into the vagina through an open speculum. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I. The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 884.9.
