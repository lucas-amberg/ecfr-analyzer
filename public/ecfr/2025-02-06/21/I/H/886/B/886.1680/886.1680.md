# ยง 886.1680   Ophthalmic projector.

(a) *Identification.* An ophthalmic projector is an AC-powered device intended to project an image on a screen for vision testing.


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 886.9.
