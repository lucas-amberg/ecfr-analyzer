# ยง 890.1450   Powered reflex hammer.

(a) *Identification.* A powered reflex hammer is a motorized device intended for medical purposes to elicit and determine controlled deep tendon reflexes.


(b) *Classification.* Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in ยง 890.9.
