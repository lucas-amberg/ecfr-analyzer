# ยง 890.5660   Therapeutic massager.

(a) *Identification.* A therapeutic massager is an electrically powered device intended for medical purposes, such as to relieve minor muscle aches and pains. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in ยง 890.9.
