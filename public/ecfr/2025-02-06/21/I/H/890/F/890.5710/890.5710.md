# ยง 890.5710   Hot or cold disposable pack.

(a) *Identification.* A hot or cold disposable pack is a device intended for medical purposes that consists of a sealed plastic bag incorporating chemicals that, upon activation, provides hot or cold therapy for body surfaces. 


(b) *Classification.* Class I (general controls). Except when intended for use on infants, the device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to ยง 890.9.
