# ยง 1315.37   Abandonment of procurement quota.

Any manufacturer assigned a procurement quota for a chemical pursuant to ยง 1315.23 may at any time abandon his right to manufacture all or any part of such quota by filing a notice of such abandonment with the UN Reporting and Quota Section, Diversion Control Division, Drug Enforcement Administration in the online Quota Management System.
