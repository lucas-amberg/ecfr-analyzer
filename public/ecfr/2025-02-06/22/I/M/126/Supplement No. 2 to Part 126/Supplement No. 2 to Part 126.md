# Supplement No. 2 to Part 126—Excluded Technology List



This supplement lists the defense articles and defense services excluded from the scope of the exemption provided at § 126.7. The United States Munitions List (USML), see 22 CFR 121.1, entries in column 1 represent the location of the excluded defense articles and defense services within the USML and does not indicate the entire USML entry in column 1 is excluded; only the portions of those entries that are further described in column 2 are excluded.
