(a) In no event may assistance be provided under this subpart for any individual for a period in excess of 45 months, or the equivalent thereof in part-time education or training. 


(b) The eligibility of a spouse for benefits under paragraph (c) or (d) of ยง 191.31 shall expire on a date which is 10 years after the date of the release of the hostage, or the death of the hostage, respectively.
