(a) Witnesses (whether appearing voluntarily, or under a subpoena) shall be paid the fee and mileage allowances which are paid subpoenaed witnesses in the courts of the United States: *Provided,* That any witness who is employed by the Federal Government shall not be entitled to receive witness fees in addition to compensation received pursuant to ยง 1429.13.
