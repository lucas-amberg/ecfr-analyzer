# ยง 490.703   Applicability.

The CMAQ Traffic Congestion performance measures are applicable to all urbanized areas that include NHS mileage and with a population over 1 million for the first performance period and in urbanized areas with a population over 200,000 for the second and all other performance periods, that are, in all or part, designated as nonattainment or maintenance areas for ozone (O_3_), carbon monoxide (CO), or particulate matter (PM_10_ and PM_2.5_) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
