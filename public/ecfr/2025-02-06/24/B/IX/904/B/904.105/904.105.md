# ยง 904.105   Counseling of homebuyers.

The LHA shall provide counseling and training as provided in subpart C of this part, with funding as provided in ยง 904.206 of this part. Applicants for admission shall be advised of the nature of the counseling and training program available to them and the application for admission shall include a statement that the family agrees to participate and cooperate fully in all official pre-occupancy and post-occupancy training and counseling activities. Failure to participate as agreed may result in the family not being selected or retained as a homebuyer.
