# ยง 960.705   Animals that assist, support, or provide service to persons with disabilities.

(a) This subpart G does not apply to animals that assist, support or provide service to persons with disabilities. PHAs may not apply or enforce any policies established under this subpart against animals that are necessary as a reasonable accommodation to assist, support or provide service to persons with disabilities. This exclusion applies to such animals that reside in public housing, as that term is used in ยง 960.703, and such animals that visit these developments. 


(b) Nothing in this subpart G: 


(1) Limits or impairs the rights of persons with disabilities; 


(2) Authorizes PHAs to limit or impair the rights of persons with disabilities; or 


(3) Affects any authority that PHAs may have to regulate service animals that assist, support or provide service to persons with disabilities, under Federal, State, or local law.
