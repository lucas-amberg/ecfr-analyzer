# ยง 583.200   Application and grant award.

When funds are made available for assistance, HUD will publish a notice of funding availability (NOFA) in the _Federal Register,_ in accordance with the requirements of 24 CFR part 4. HUD will review and screen applications in accordance with the requirements in section 426 of the McKinney Act (42 U.S.C. 11386) and the guidelines, rating criteria, and procedures published in the NOFA.
