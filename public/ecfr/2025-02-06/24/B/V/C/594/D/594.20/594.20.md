# ยง 594.20   Submission procedures.

(a) *Use of NOFAs.* The Department will publish a Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) in the _Federal Register_ for each funding competition under this program, indicating the objective of the competition; the amount of funding available; the application procedures, including the eligible applicants and activities to be funded; and any special conditions applicable to the competition, including the requirements for the match. The NOFA also will describe the maximum points to be awarded under each evaluation criterion, for the purpose of ranking applications, and any special factors to be considered in assigning the points to each criterion. 


(b) Applications shall be submitted in accordance with the time, place, and content described in the NOFA.
