# ยง 888.202   Manner of publication.

Adjustment Factors will be published in the _Federal Register_ at least annually by Notice. Interim revisions may be published as market conditions indicate. In the case of revised factors applicable only to specific areas, the HUD Field Office will publish a notice appropriate to the limited scope of the revised factors (see ยง 888.204).
