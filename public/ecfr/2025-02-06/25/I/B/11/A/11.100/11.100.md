# ยง 11.100   Where are Courts of Indian Offenses established?

(a) A list of the areas in Indian Country where Courts of Indian Offenses are established is available on the Bureau of Indian Affairs website (*www.bia.gov*) and is published periodically in the _Federal Register_.


(b) The Director, Bureau of Indian Affairs, will maintain on the Bureau of Indian Affairs website (*www.bia.gov*) an updated list of the areas in Indian Country where Courts of Indian Offenses are established and, upon any change to the list, will publish notice of the change in the _Federal Register_ with an updated complete list.
