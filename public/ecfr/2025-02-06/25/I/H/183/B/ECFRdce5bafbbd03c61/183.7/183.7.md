# § 183.7   What would cause the Secretary to disapprove a request?

We will only disapprove a request for the distribution of principal or income from the Trust Fund if the request does any of the following: 


(a) Fails to provide the documents identified in § 183.5; 


(b) Fails to provide reports required under §§ 183.15 and 183.16; or 


(c) Includes a use requested or written budget that does not comply with a specific provision of the Settlement Act, or this part.
