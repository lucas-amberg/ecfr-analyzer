# § 224.71   What standards will the Secretary use to decide to approve a final proposed TERA?

The Secretary must approve a final proposed TERA unless:


(a) The Tribe does not meet the definition of a “qualified Tribe” in § 224.30;


(b) A provision of the TERA violates applicable Federal law (including regulations) or a treaty applicable to the Tribe; or


(c) The TERA fails to include the provisions required by § 224.63.
