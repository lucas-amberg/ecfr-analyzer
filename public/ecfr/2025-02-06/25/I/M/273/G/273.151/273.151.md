# ยง 273.151   What information must the existing contracting party provide in the annual report?

Existing contracting parties who receive Johnson-O'Malley funding must submit the following information in the annual report:


(a) General information about the contractor;


(b) General information about the number and names of the schools;


(c) The number of eligible Indian students who were served using amounts allocated under the contract during the previous fiscal year;


(d) An accounting of the amounts and purposes for which the contract funds were expended;


(e) Information on the conduct of the program;


(f) A quantitative evaluation of the effectiveness of the contract program in meeting the stated objectives contained in the educational plans; and


(g) A complete accounting of actual receipts at the end of the fiscal year for which the contract funds were expended.
