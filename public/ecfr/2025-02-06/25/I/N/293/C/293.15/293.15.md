# ยง 293.15   When may the Secretary disapprove a compact or amendment?

The Secretary may disapprove a compact or amendment only if:


(a) It violates:


(1) Any provision of IGRA;


(2) Any other provision of Federal law that does not relate to jurisdiction over gaming on Indian lands; or


(3) The trust obligations of the United States to Indians; or


(b) The documents required in ยง 293.8 are not submitted and the parties have been informed in writing of the missing documents and are provided with an opportunity to supply those documents.
