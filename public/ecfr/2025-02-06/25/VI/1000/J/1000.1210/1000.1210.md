# ยง 1000.1210   When can a Tribe/Consortium request a waiver of a regulation?

A Tribe/Consortium may request a waiver of a regulation:


(a) As part of the negotiation process;


(b) At any time after a funding agreement has been executed; or


(c) Following a denial decision, provided that the Tribe/Consortium acknowledges that the submission commences a new 120-day review period under ยง 1000.1240.
