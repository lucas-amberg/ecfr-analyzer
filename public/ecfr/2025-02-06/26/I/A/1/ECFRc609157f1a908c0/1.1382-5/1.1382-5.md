# ยง 1.1382-5   Taxable income of cooperatives; products marketed under pooling arrangements.

For purposes of section 1382(b) and ยง 1.1382-2, in the case of a pooling arrangement for the marketing of products the patronage under such pool shall be treated as occurring during the taxable year in which the pool closes. The determination of when a pool is closed will be made on the basis of the facts and circumstances in each case, but generally the practices and operations of the cooperative organization shall control.
