# ยง 1.337-1   Nonrecognition for property distributed to parent in complete liquidation of subsidiary.

(a) *General rule.* If sections 332(a) and 337 are applicable with respect to the receipt of a subsidiary`s property in complete liquidation, no gain or loss is recognized to the liquidating subsidiary with respect to such property (including property distributed with respect to indebtedness, see section 337(b)(1) and ยง 1.332-7), except as provided in section 337(b)(2) (distributions to certain tax-exempt distributees), section 367(e)(2) (distributions to foreign corporations), and section 897(d) (distributions of U.S. real property interests by foreign corporations).


(b) *Applicability date.* This section applies to any taxable year beginning on or after March 28, 2016.
