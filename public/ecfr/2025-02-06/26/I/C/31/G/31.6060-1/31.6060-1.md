# ยง 31.6060-1   Reporting requirements for tax return preparers.

(a) *In general.* A person that employs one or more tax return preparers to prepare a return or claim for refund of employment tax under chapters 21 through 25 of subtitle C of the Internal Revenue Code, other than for the person, at any time during a return period, shall satisfy the recordkeeping and inspection requirements in the manner stated in ยง 1.6060-1 of this chapter.


(b) *Effective/applicability date.* This section is applicable to returns and claims for refund filed after December 31, 2008.
