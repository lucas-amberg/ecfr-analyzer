# ยง 301.6223(f)-1   Duplicate copy of final partnership administrative adjustment.

(a) *In general.* Section 6223(f) does not prohibit the Internal Revenue Service from issuing a duplicate copy of the notice of final partnership administrative adjustment (for example, in the event the original notice is lost). 


(b) *Effective date.* This section is applicable to partnership taxable years beginning on or after October 4, 2001. For years beginning prior to October 4, 2001, see ยง 301.6223(f)-1T contained in 26 CFR part 1, revised April 1, 2001.
