# § 21.124-T   Raffinate.

(a) *API Gravity at 60 °F.* 30 to 85.


(b) *Reid Vapor Pressure (PSI).* 5 to 11.


(c) *Octane (R+M/2).* 66 to 70.


(d) *Distillation ( °F):*

(i) *10 percent.* 120 to 150.


(ii) *50 percent.* 144 to 180.


(iii) *90 percent.* 168 to 200.


(iv) *End point distillation.* 216 to 285.
