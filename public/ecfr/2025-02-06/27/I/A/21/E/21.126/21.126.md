# § 21.126   Safrole.

(a) *Congealing point.* 10.0° to 11.2 °C.


(b) *Refractive index at 20 °C.* 1.5363 to 1.5385.


(c) *Specific gravity at 15 °/15 °C.* 1.100 to 1.107.


(d) *Odor.* Characteristic odor.
