# § 21.129   Spearmint oil, terpeneless.

(a) *Carvone content.* Not less than 85 percent by weight.


(b) *Refractive index at 20 °C.* 1.4930 to 1.4980.


(c) *Specific gravity at 25 °/25 °C.* 0.949 to 0.956.


(d) *Odor.* Characteristic odor.
