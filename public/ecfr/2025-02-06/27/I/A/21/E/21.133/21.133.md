# ยง 21.133   Vinegar.

(a) *Vinegar, 90-grain:*

*Acidity (as acetic acid).* 9.0 percent by weight, minimum.


(b) *Vinegar, 60-grain:*

*Acidity (as acetic acid).* 6.0 percent by weight, minimum.
