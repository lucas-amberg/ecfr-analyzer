# § 22.93   Equipment for recovery and restoration of tax-free alcohol.

(a) *Location.* All equipment used to recover and restore tax-free alcohol for reuse shall be located on the permit premises.


(b) *Construction.* (1) Distilling apparatus, pipelines and other equipment used for recovery and restoration of tax-free alcohol shall be constructed and secured in such a manner as to prevent unauthorized access and so arranged as to be readily inspected.


(2) Storage tanks shall be provided for the collection of recovered tax-free alcohol. Each storage tank shall—


(i) Be durably marked as to use and capacity;


(ii) Be equipped with, or for, an accurate means of measuring the spirits; and


(iii) Be equipped for locking to control unauthorized access to the spirits.
