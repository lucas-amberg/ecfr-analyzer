# § 26.114   Permit to ship required.

Before liquors and articles of Puerto Rican manufacture, upon which all internal revenue taxes have been paid or deferred as prescribed in this subpart, may be shipped to the United States, a permit to ship, form TTB F 5170.7, must be obtained from the Secretary as provided in §§ 26.115 and 26.116.
