# ยง 27.175   Receipt of distilled spirits by consignee.

Proprietors of distilled spirits plants who receive imported spirits under this subpart shall follow the requirements in 27 CFR part 19 for spirits received by transfer in bond. Proprietors of distilled spirits plants may receive such imported spirits even if they are exempt from bond requirements under ยง 19.151(d) of this chapter. However, proprietors are not required to file application on TTB Form 5100.16 to receive imported spirits from customs custody.
