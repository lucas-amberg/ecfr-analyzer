# ยง 44.123   Amount of bond.

The amount of the bond filed by the export warehouse proprietor, as required by ยง 44.86, shall be not less than the estimated amount of tax which may at any time constitute a charge against the bond: *Provided,* That the amount of any such bond (or the total amount where original and strengthening bonds are filed) shall not exceed $200,000 nor be less than $1,000.
