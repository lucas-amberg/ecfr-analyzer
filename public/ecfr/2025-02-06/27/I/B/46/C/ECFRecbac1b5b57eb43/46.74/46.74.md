# § 46.74   Execution of claims.

Disaster loss claims for tobacco products or cigarette papers or tubes must be executed on form TTB F 5620.8, Claim—Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms Taxes, in accordance with the instructions on the form. If a claim involves taxes on both domestic and imported products, the quantities of each must be shown separately in the claim. Prepare a separate claim in respect of customs duties.
