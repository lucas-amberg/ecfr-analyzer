# § 555.23   List of explosive materials.

The Director shall compile a list of explosive materials, which shall be published and revised at least annually in the _Federal Register._ The “List of Explosive Materials” (ATF Publication 5400.8) is available at no cost upon request from the ATF Distribution Center (See § 555.21).
