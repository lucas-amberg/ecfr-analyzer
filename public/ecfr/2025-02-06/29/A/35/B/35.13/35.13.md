# ยง 35.13   Exceptions to the rules against age discrimination: reasonable factors other than age.

A recipient is permitted to take an action otherwise prohibited by ยง 35.10, if that action is based on a factor other than age, even though the action may have a disproportionate effect on persons of different ages.
