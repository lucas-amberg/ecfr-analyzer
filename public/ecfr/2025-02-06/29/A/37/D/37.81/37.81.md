# ยง 37.81   Are there any circumstances under which the Director may extend the time limit for filing a complaint with him or her?

Yes. The Director may extend the 30-day time limit:


(a) If the recipient does not include in its Notice of Final Action the required notice about the complainant's right to file with the Director, as described in ยง 37.76(b)(5)(ii); or


(b) For other good cause shown.


The complainant has the burden of proving to the Director that the time limit should be extended.
