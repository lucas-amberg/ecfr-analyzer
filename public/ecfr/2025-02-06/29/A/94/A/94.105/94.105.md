# § 94.105   Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either— 


(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of Labor; or 


(2) A(n) Department of Labor awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in §§ 94.605 and 94.660, respectively.)
