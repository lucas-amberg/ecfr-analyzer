# ยง 457.10   CSRA; FSA; CAA; LMRDA.

*CSRA* means the Civil Service Reform Act of 1978; *FSA* means the Foreign Service Act of 1980; *CAA* means the Congressional Accountability Act of 1995; *LMRDA* means the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959, as amended.
