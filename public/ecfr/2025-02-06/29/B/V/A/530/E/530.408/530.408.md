# ยง 530.408   Notice of the Secretary to review decision.

Whenever the Administrative Review Board determines to review the decision and order of an Administrative Law Judge, the Secretary shall notify each party of the issue or issues raised; the form in which submission shall be made (*i.e.,* briefs, oral argument, etc.); and, the time within which such presentation shall be submitted.
