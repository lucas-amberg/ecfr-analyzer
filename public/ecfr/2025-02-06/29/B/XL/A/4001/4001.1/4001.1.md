# ยง 4001.1   Purpose and scope.

(a) *In general.* This part contains definitions of certain terms used in this chapter and the regulations under which the PBGC makes various controlled group determinations. 


(b) *Title IV coverage.* Coverage by section 4050 of ERISA is not and does not result in or confer coverage by title IV of ERISA.
