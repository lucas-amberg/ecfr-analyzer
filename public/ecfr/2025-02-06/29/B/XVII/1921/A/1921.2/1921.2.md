# ยง 1921.2   Definitions.

(a) *Act* means the Longshoremen's and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act. 


(b) *Chief Hearing Examiner* means the Chief Hearing Examiner, United States Department of Labor, Washington DC 20210. 


(c) *Respondent* means the person or organization proceeded against. 


(d) *Assistant Secretary* means the Assistant Secretary for Occupational Safety and Health.
