# ยง 2700.52   Expedition of proceedings.

(a) *Motions.* In addition to making a written motion pursuant to ยง 2700.10, a party may request expedition of proceedings by oral motion, with concurrent notice to all parties. Oral motions shall be reduced to writing within 24 hours.


(b) *Timing of hearing.* Unless all parties consent to an earlier hearing, an expedited hearing on the merits of the case shall not be held on less than 4 days notice.
