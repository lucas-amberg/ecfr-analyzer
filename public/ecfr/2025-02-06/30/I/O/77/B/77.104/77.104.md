# ยง 77.104   Repair of energized surface high-voltage lines; qualified person.

An individual is a qualified person within the meaning of ยง 77.704 of this part for the purpose of repairing energized surface high-voltage lines only if he has had at least 2 years experience in electrical maintenance, and at least 2 years experience in the repair of energized high-voltage lines located on poles or structures.
