# ยง 285.401   When may BSEE issue a cessation order?

(a) BSEE may issue a cessation order during the term of your lease or grant when you fail to comply with an applicable law; regulation; order; or provision of a lease, grant, plan, or BSEE or BOEM approval. Except as provided in ยง 285.400(e), BSEE will allow you a period of time to correct any noncompliance before issuing an order to cease activities.


(b) A cessation order will set forth what measures you are required to take, including reports you are required to prepare and submit to BSEE, to receive approval to resume activities on your lease or grant.
