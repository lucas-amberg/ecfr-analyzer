# ยง 291.104   Who may file a complaint or a third-party brief?

(a) You may file a complaint under this subpart if you are a shipper and you believe that you have been denied open and nondiscriminatory access to an OCSLA pipeline that is not a FERC pipeline.


(b) Any person that believes its interests may be affected by precedents established by adjudication of complaints under this rule may submit a brief to BSEE. The brief must be served following the procedure set out in ยง 291.107. After considering the brief, it is within BSEE's discretion as to whether BSEE may:


(1) Address the brief in its decision;


(2) Not address the brief in its decision; or


(3) Include the submitter of the brief in the proceeding as a party.
