# ยง 550.126   Electronic payment instructions.

You must file all payments electronically through *Pay.gov.* This includes, but is not limited to, all OCS applications or filing fee payments. The *Pay.gov* Web site may be accessed through *Pay.gov* at *https://www.pay.gov/paygov/.*

(a) [Reserved]


(b) You must use credit card or automated clearing house (ACH) payments through the *Pay.gov* Web site, and you must include a copy of the *Pay.gov* confirmation receipt page with your application.
