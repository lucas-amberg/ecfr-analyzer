# ยง 556.802   When may BOEM disapprove the transfer of all or part of my operating rights interest?

BOEM may disapprove a transfer of all or part of your operating rights interest:


(a) When the transferor or transferee is not in compliance with all applicable regulations and orders, including financial assurance requirements;


(b) When a transferor attempts a transfer that is not acceptable as to form or content (*e.g.,* not on standard form, containing incorrect legal description, not executed in accordance with corporate governance, transferee does not meet the requirements of ยง 556.401); or
