# ยง 1210.101   Who must submit production reports?

(a) If you operate a Federal or Indian oil and gas lease or federally approved unit or communitization agreement, you must submit production reports.


(b) Before reporting production to ONRR, you must obtain an operator number. To obtain an operator number, refer to the ONRR *Minerals Production Reporter Handbook* for instructions and ONRR contact information (also see ยง 1210.106 for information on how to obtain a handbook).
