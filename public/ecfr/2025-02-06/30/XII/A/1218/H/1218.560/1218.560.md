# ยง 1218.560   How do I submit Form ONRR-4444?

You may obtain a copy of Form ONRR-4444 and instructions from ONRR. This form is posted at *http://www.onrr.gov/FM/Forms/default.htm.* Submit the completed, signed form to the address designated on Form ONRR-4444 instructions.
