# ยง 1227.104   What will ONRR do when it receives a State's delegation proposal?

When ONRR receives your delegation proposal, it will record the receipt date. ONRR will notify you in writing within 15 business days whether your proposal is complete. If it is not complete, ONRR will identify any missing items ยง 1227.103 requires. Once you submit all required information, ONRR will notify you of the date your application is complete.
