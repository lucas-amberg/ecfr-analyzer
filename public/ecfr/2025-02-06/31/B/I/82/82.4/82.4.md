(a) Any person who exports, melts, or treats 5-cent coins or one-cent coins of the United States in violation of ยง 82.1 shall be subject to the penalties specified in 31 U.S.C. 5111(d), including a fine of not more than $10,000 and/or imprisonment of not more than 5 years.
