# ยง 321.23   Paying agent fees and charges.

(a) *Fees.* Fees may be paid as outlined in this section. A schedule setting out the fees, and the basis on which they are computed and paid, is separately published in the _Federal Register._ Current information is available from a TRS Site or online at *www.treasurydirect.gov.*

(b) *Discontinuance, reduction, or delay in fees.* The Secretary may authorize, upon notice in the _Federal Register,_ the discontinuance, reduction, or delay of fee payments.


(c) *Charges to presenters.* A paying agent shall not make any charge whatever to persons entitled to request payment of securities, for redeeming them under the provisions of this part.
