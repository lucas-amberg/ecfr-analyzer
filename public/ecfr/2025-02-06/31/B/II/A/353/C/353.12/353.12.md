# ยง 353.12   Disposition of excess.

If any person at any time has savings bonds issued during any one calendar year in excess of the prescribed amount, the Bureau of the Fiscal Service reserves the right to take any action that it deems necessary to adjust the excess. Instructions for adjustment of the excess can be obtained by email at *savbonds@bpd.treas.gov* or by writing to Bureau of the Fiscal Service, Parkersburg, WV 26106-1328.
