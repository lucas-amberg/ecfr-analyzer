# ยง 525.202   Prohibited transactions related to financial services to or for the benefit of Myanma Oil and Gas Enterprise (MOGE) (Directive 1).

The following activities by a U.S. person are prohibited on or after December 15, 2023: The provision, exportation, or reexportation, directly or indirectly, of financial services to or for the benefit of the Myanma Oil and Gas Enterprise (MOGE) or its property or interests in property.
