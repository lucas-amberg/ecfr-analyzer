# ยง 542.318   Person subject to United States sanctions concerning Syria.

The term *person subject to United States sanctions concerning Syria,* as used in ยง 542.212, means:


(a) Any person, including the Government of Syria, with whom transactions are restricted pursuant to any Executive order relating to the national emergency declared in E.O. 13338; or


(b) Any person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (50 U.S.C. 1701 *et seq.*) in connection with Syria's proliferation of weapons of mass destruction or delivery systems for weapons of mass destruction, or Syria's support for international terrorism.
