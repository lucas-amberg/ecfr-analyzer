# § 546.405   Provision of services.

(a) The prohibitions on transactions involving blocked property contained in § 546.201 apply to services performed in the United States or by U.S. persons, wherever located, including by an overseas branch of an entity located in the United States:


(1) On behalf of or for the benefit of a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to § 546.201; or


(2) With respect to property interests subject to § 546.201.


(b) *Example.* U.S. persons may not, except as authorized by or pursuant to this part, provide legal, accounting, financial, brokering, freight forwarding, transportation, public relations, or other services to a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to § 546.201.
