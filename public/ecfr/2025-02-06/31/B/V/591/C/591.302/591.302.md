# ยง 591.302   Effective date.

The term *effective date* refers to the effective date of the applicable prohibitions and directives contained in this part as follows:


(a) With respect to a person listed in the Annex to E.O. 13692 of March 8, 2015, 12:01 a.m. eastern daylight time, March 9, 2015; and


(b) With respect to a person whose property and interests in property are otherwise blocked pursuant to ยง 591.201, the earlier of the date of actual or constructive notice that such person's property and interests in property are blocked.
