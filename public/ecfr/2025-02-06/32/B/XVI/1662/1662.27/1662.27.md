# ยง 1662.27   Where records are published.

Materials SSS is required to publish pursuant to the provisions of 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(1) and (a)(2) are published in one of the following ways:


(a) By publication in the _Federal Register_ of Selective Service System regulations, and by their subsequent inclusion in the Code of Federal Regulations;


(b) By publication in the _Federal Register_ of appropriate general notices; and/or


(c) By other forms of publication, when incorporated by reference in the _Federal Register_ with the approval of the Director of the Federal Register.
