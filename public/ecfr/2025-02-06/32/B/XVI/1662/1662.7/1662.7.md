# ยง 1662.7   Where to submit a FOIA request.

*Submission of requests.* Requesters must submit FOIA requests in writing to the Agency through one of the following options:


(a) *Online FOIA portal:* link available from the Agency's *www.sss.gov/foia* website.


(b) *Email: FOIA@sss.gov.*

(c) *Mail:* SSS, ATTN: Freedom of Information Act Officer, 1501 Wilson Boulevard, Suite 700, Arlington, VA 22209.
