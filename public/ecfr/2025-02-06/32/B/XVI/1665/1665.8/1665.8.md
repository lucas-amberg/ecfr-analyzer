# ยง 1665.8   Systems of records exempted from certain provisions of this act.

The SSS will not provide requesters information exempt from disclosure pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552a(k), (*e.g.,* the SSS will not reveal to the suspected violator the informant's name or other identifying information relating to the informant).
