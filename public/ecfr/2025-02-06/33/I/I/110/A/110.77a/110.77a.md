# § 110.77a   Duluth-Superior Harbor, Duluth, Minn.

The area adjacent to Park Point in Duluth-Superior Harbor within the following boundaries: beginning at latitude 46°45′19.3″ N., longitude 92°04′43″ W.; thence to latitude 46°45′11.7″ N., longitude 92°05′01″ W.; thence to latitude 46°44′21.2″ N., longitude 92°04′15.7″ W.; thence to latitude 46°44′29.4″ N., longitude 92°03′57.5″ W.; thence to the point of beginning.
