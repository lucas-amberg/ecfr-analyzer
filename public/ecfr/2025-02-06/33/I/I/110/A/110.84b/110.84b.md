# § 110.84b   Buffalo, N.Y.

The area within the Port of Buffalo known as Port of Buffalo Small Boat Harbor commencing at a point on shore at latitude 42°51′05″ N., longitude 78°51′55″ W.; thence 240° to rip-rap dike thence following the dike to the shoreline; thence along the shoreline to the point of origin.
