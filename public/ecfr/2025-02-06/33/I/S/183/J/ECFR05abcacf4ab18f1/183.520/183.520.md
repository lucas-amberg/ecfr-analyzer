# ยง 183.520   Fuel tank vent systems.

(a) Each fuel tank must have a vent system that prevents pressure in the tank from exceeding 80 percent of the pressure marked on the tank label under ยง 183.514(b)(5).


(b) Each vent must:


(1) Have a flame arrester that can be cleaned unless the vent is itself a flame arrestor; and


(2) Not allow a fuel overflow at the rate of up to two gallons per minute to enter the boat.
