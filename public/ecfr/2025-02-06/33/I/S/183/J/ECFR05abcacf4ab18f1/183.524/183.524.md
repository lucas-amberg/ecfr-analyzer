# ยง 183.524   Fuel pumps.

(a) Each diaphragm pump must not leak fuel from the pump if the primary diaphragm fails.


(b) Each electrically operated fuel pump must not operate except when the engine is operating or when the engine is started.


(c) If tested under ยง 183.590, each fuel pump, as installed in the boat, must not leak more than five ounces of fuel in 2
1/2 minutes, inclusive of leaks from fuel line, fuel filter and strainer.
