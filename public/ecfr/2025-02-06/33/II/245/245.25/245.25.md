# ยง 245.25   Determination of remedial action.

(a) *Consultation with Coast Guard.* After a determination has been made that an obstruction presents a hazard to navigation, District Engineers will consult with the Coast Guard district to determine appropriate remedial action for the specific situation.


(b) *Options.* The following options, or some combination of these options, may be considered:


(1) No action.


(2) Charting.


(3) Broadcast notice to mariners and publication of navigational safety information.


(4) Marking.


(5) Redefinition of navigational area (e.g., channel, fairway, anchorage, etc.).


(6) Removal.
