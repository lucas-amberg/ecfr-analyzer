# ยง 403.3   Definitions. [Rule 3]

In these rules, unless the context otherwise requires:


(a) *Application* includes complaint;


(b) *Affidavit* includes a written affirmation;


(c) *Board* means the Joint Tolls Review Board;


(d) Words in the singular include the plural and words in the plural include the singular [Rule 3].
