# ยง 462.4   What are the transition rules for using tests to measure educational gain for the National Reporting System for Adult Education (NRS)?

A State or an eligible provider may continue to measure educational gain for the NRS using tests that the Secretary has identified in the most recent notice published in the _Federal Register_ until the Secretary announces through a notice published in the _Federal Register_ a date by which such tests may no longer be used.
