# ยง 668.121   Final decision of the Department.

(a) In the event that the initial decision of the hearing official is appealed, the decision of the Secretary is the final decision of the Department, unless the hearing official's decision is remanded by the Secretary.


(b) In the event that the initial decision of the hearing official is not appealed within the time limit specified in ยง 668.119(a), the initial decision automatically becomes the final decision of the Department.
