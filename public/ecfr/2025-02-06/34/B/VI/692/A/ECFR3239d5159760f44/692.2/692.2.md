# § 692.2   Who is eligible to participate in the LEAP Program?

(a) *State participation.* A State that meets the requirements in §§ 692.20 and 692.21 is eligible to receive payments under the LEAP program.


(b) *Student participation.* A student must meet the requirements of § 692.40 to be eligible to receive assistance from a State under the LEAP program.
