# ยง 13.1906   Headquarters/Visitor Center Developed Area (HVCDA).

For purposes of this subpart, the HVCDA consists of all park areas within a 
1/2 mile radius of the Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve Headquarters building, other than the Valdez Trail. The following activities are prohibited within the HVCDA:


(a) Lighting or maintaining a fire;


(b) Camping;


(c) Entering the area after visiting hours (visiting hours will be posted at the entrance gate).
