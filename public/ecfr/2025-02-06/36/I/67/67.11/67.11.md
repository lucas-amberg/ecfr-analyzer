# ยง 67.11   Fees for processing certification requests.

(a) Fees are charged for reviewing certification requests according to the schedule and instructions provided in public notices in the _Federal Register_ by NPS.


(b) No payment should be made until requested by the NPS. A certification decision will not be issued on an application until the appropriate remittance is received.


(c) Fees are nonrefundable.
