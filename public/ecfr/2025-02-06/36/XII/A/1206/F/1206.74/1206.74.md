# ยง 1206.74   Do I need prior written approval for changes to the grant project?

You must obtain prior written approval from the NHPRC for most changes in the grant project and terms of the grant. Detailed instructions are found in *How to Administer an NHPRC Grant* available at *http://www.archives.gov/NHPRC* or from the NHPRC staff.
