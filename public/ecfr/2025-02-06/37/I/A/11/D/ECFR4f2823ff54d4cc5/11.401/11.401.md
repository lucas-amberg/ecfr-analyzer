# ยง 11.401   Truthfulness in statements to others.

In the course of representing a client, a practitioner shall not knowingly:


(a) Make a false statement of material fact or law to a third person; or


(b) Fail to disclose a material fact to a third person when disclosure is necessary to avoid assisting a criminal or fraudulent act by a client, unless disclosure is prohibited by ยง 11.106.
