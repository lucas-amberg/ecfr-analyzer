# ยง 38.765   Recipient reporting requirements.

(a) *Final report.* The recipient must submit to VA, no later than 120 calendar days after the end date of the period of performance, all financial, performance, and other reports as required by the terms and conditions of the Federal award.


(b) *Additional reporting.* Additional reporting requirements may be requested by VA to allow VA to assess program effectiveness.
