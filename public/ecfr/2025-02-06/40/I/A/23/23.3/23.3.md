# ยง 23.3   Timing of Administrator's action under Clean Air Act.

Unless the Administrator otherwise explicitly provides in a particular promulgation, approval, or action, the time and date of such promulgation, approval or action for purposes of the second sentence of section 307(b)(1) shall be at 1:00 p.m. eastern time (standard or daylight, as appropriate) on (a) for a _Federal Register_ document, the date when the document is published in the _Federal Register,_ or (b) for any other document, two weeks after it is signed.
