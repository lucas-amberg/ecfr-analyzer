# ยง 51.913   How do the section 182(f) NO

(a) A person may petition the Administrator for an exemption from NO_X_ obligations under section 182(f) for any area designated nonattainment for the 8-hour ozone NAAQS and for any area in a section 184 ozone transport region.


(b) The petition must contain adequate documentation that the criteria in section 182(f) are met.


(c) A section 182(f) NO_X_ exemption granted for the 1-hour ozone standard does not relieve the area from any NO_X_ obligations under section 182(f) for the 8-hour ozone standard.
