# ยง 52.331   Committal SIP for the Colorado Group II PM

On April 14, 1989, the Governor submitted a Committal SIP for the Colorado Group II PM_10_ areas. The SIP commits the State to continue to monitor for PM_10_, report data and to submit a full SIP if a violation of the PM_10_ National Ambient Air Quality Standards is detected.
