# ยง 52.834   Control strategy: Sulfur dioxide.

(a) *Approval.* On April 21, 1997, the Iowa Department of Natural Resources (IDNR) submitted a maintenance plan and redesignation request for the Muscatine County nonattainment area for the 1971 SO_2_ national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS). The maintenance plan and redesignation request satisfy all applicable requirements of the Clean Air Act.


(b) *Determination of attainment by the attainment date.* As of May 9, 2022, the EPA has determined that the Muscatine, Iowa SO_2_ nonattainment area has attained the 2010 1-hour SO_2_ primary NAAQS by the applicable attainment date of October 4, 2018.
