# ยง 52.2182   PM

On July 12 1988, the State submitted a Committal SIP for the Rapid City Group II PM_10_ area, as required by the PM_10_ implementation policy. The SIP commits the State to continue to monitor for PM_10_ and to submit a full SIP if a violation of the PM_10_ National Ambient Air Quality Standards is detected. It also commits the State to make several revisions related to PM_10_ to the existing SIP.
