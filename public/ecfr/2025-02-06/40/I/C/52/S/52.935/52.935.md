# ยง 52.935   PM

On July 7, 1988, the State submitted a committal SIP for the cities of Ashland and Catlettsburg in Boyd County. The committal SIP contains all the requirements identified in the July 1, 1987, promulgation of the SIP requirements for PM_10_ at 52 FR 24681. The SIP commits the State to submit an emissions inventory, continue to monitor for PM_10_, report data and to submit a full SIP if a violation of the PM_10_ National Ambient Air Quality Standards is detected.
