*Nitrogen oxides.* On and after the date on which the performance test required to be conducted by ยง 60.73a(e) is completed, you may not discharge into the atmosphere from any affected facility any gases which contain NO_X_, expressed as NO_2_, in excess of 0.50 pounds (lb) per ton of nitric acid produced, as a 30-day emission rate calculated based on 30 consecutive operating days, the production being expressed as 100 percent nitric acid.
