# § 60.5550a   What notifications must I submit and when?

(a) You must prepare and submit the notifications specified in §§ 60.7(a)(1) and (3) and 60.19, as applicable to your affected EGU(s) (see table 3 to this subpart).


(b) You must prepare and submit notifications specified in 40 CFR 75.61, as applicable, to your affected EGUs.
