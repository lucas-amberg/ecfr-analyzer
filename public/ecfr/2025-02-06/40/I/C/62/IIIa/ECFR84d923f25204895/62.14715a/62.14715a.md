# ยง 62.14715a   What must I include in the deviation report?

In each report required under ยง 62.14710a, for any pollutant or parameter that deviated from the emission limitations or operating limits specified in this subpart, include the four items described in paragraphs (a) through (d) of this section.


(a) The calendar dates and times your unit deviated from the emission limitations or operating limit requirements;


(b) The averaged and recorded data for the dates in paragraph (a) of this section;


(c) Duration and causes of the following:


(1) Each deviation from the emission limitations or operating limits and your corrective actions; and


(2) Bypass events and your corrective actions. Include the calendar date and start time of the bypass event; and


(d) A copy of the operating limit monitoring data during each deviation and, for any test report that documents the emission levels, the process unit(s) tested, the pollutant(s) tested and the date that the performance test was conducted.
