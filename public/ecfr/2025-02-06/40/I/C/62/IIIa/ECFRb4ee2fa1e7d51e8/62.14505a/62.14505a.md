# ยง 62.14505a   What are the principal components of this subpart?

This subpart contains the ten major components listed in paragraphs (a) through (j) of this section.


(a) Waste management plan.


(b) Operator training and qualification.


(c) Emission limitations and operating limits.


(d) Performance testing.


(e) Initial compliance requirements.


(f) Continuous compliance requirements.


(g) Monitoring.


(h) Recordkeeping and reporting.


(i) Definitions.


(j) Tables.
