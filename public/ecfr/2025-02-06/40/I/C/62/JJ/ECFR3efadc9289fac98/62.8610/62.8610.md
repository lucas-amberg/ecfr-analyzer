# § 62.8610   Identification of plan—Negative declaration.

The State of North Dakota submitted a letter on May 8, 2019 certifying that there are no designated facilities subject to the emission guidelines for existing hospital medical infectious waste incinerators under 40 CFR part 60, subpart Ce operating within the State's jurisdiction.
