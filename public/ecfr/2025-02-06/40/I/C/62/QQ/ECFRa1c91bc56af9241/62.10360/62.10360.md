# ยง 62.10360   Identification of plan.

The State of South Dakota submitted a letter on June 7, 2021, certifying that there are no designated facilities subject to the emissions guidelines for existing hospital medical infectious waste incinerators under 40 CFR part 60, subpart Ce, operating within the State's jurisdiction.
