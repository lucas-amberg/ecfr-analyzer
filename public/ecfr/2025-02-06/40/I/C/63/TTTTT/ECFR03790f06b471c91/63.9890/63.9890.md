# ยง 63.9890   What emission limitations must I meet?

(a) You must meet each emission limit in Table 1 to this subpart that applies to you.


(b) For each wet scrubber applied to meet any particulate matter, particulate matter less than 10 microns (PM_10_), chlorine, hydrochloric acid, or dioxins/furans emission limit in Table 1 to this subpart, you must maintain the hourly average pressure drop and scrubber liquid flow rate at or above the minimum level established during the initial or subsequent performance test.
