# ยง 63.10434   In what form and for how long must I keep my records?

(a) Your records must be in a form suitable and readily available for expeditious review.


(b) You must keep each record for 5 years following the date of each record.


(c) You must keep each record onsite for at least 2 years after the date of each record. You may keep the records offsite for the remaining 3 years.
