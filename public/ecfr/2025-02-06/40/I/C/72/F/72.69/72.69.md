# ยง 72.69   Issuance and effective date of acid rain permits.

(a) After the close of the public comment period, the Administrator will issue or deny an Acid Rain permit. The Administrator will serve a copy of any Acid Rain permit and the response to comments on the designated representative for the source covered by the issuance or denial and serve written notice of the issuance or denial on the air pollution control agencies of affected States and any interested person. The Administrator will also give notice in the _Federal Register._

(b)(1) The term of every Acid Rain permit shall be 5 years commencing on its effective date.


(2) Every Acid Rain permit for Phase I shall take effect on January 1, 1995.
