# ยง 96.352   Responsibilities of CAIR authorized account representative.

Following the establishment of a CAIR NO_X_ Ozone Season Allowance Tracking System account, all submissions to the Administrator pertaining to the account, including, but not limited to, submissions concerning the deduction or transfer of CAIR NO_X_ Ozone Season allowances in the account, shall be made only by the CAIR authorized account representative for the account.
