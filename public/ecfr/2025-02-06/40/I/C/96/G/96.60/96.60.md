# ยง 96.60   Submission of NO

The NO_X_ authorized account representatives seeking recordation of a NO_X_ allowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator. To be considered correctly submitted, the NO_X_ allowance transfer shall include the following elements in a format specified by the Administrator:


(a) The numbers identifying both the transferor and transferee accounts;


(b) A specification by serial number of each NO_X_ allowance to be transferred; and


(c) The printed name and signature of the NO_X_ authorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed.
