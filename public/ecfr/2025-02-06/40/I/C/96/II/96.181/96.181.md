# § 96.181   General.

(a) Except as otherwise provided in §§ 96.101 through 96.104, §§ 96.106 through 96.108, and subparts BB and CC and subparts FF through HH of this part, a CAIR NO_X_ opt-in unit shall be treated as a CAIR NO_X_ unit for purposes of applying such sections and subparts of this part.


(b) Solely for purposes of applying, as provided in this subpart, the requirements of subpart HH of this part to a unit for which a CAIR opt-in permit application is submitted and not withdrawn and a CAIR opt-in permit is not yet issued or denied under this subpart, such unit shall be treated as a CAIR NO_X_ unit before issuance of a CAIR opt-in permit for such unit.
