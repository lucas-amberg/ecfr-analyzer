# ยง 97.160   Submission of CAIR NO

A CAIR authorized account representative seeking recordation of a CAIR NO_X_ allowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator. To be considered correctly submitted, the CAIR NO_X_ allowance transfer shall include the following elements, in a format specified by the Administrator:


(a) The account numbers for both the transferor and transferee accounts;


(b) The serial number of each CAIR NO_X_ allowance that is in the transferor account and is to be transferred; and


(c) The name and signature of the CAIR authorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed.
