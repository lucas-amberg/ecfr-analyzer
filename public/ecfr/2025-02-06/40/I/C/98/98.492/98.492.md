# ยง 98.492   GHGs to report.

You must report:


(a) CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O emissions from each coke calcining unit under this subpart.


(b) CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O emissions from auxiliary fuel used in the coke calcining unit and afterburner, if applicable, or other control system used to treat the coke calcining unit's process off-gas under subpart C of this part by following the requirements of subpart C.
