# ยง 98.502   GHGs to report.

You must report:


(a) Process CO_2_ emissions from each calcium carbide process unit or furnace used for the production of calcium carbide.


(b) CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O emissions from each stationary combustion unit following the requirements of subpart C of this part. You must report these emissions under subpart C of this part by following the requirements of subpart C.
