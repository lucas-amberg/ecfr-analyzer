# ยง 98.282   GHGs to report.

You must report:


(a) CO_2_ process emissions from all silicon carbide process units or furnaces combined.


(b) CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O emissions from each stationary combustion unit. You must report these emissions under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) by following the requirements of subpart C.
