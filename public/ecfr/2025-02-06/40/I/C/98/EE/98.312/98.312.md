# ยง 98.312   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report CO_2_ process emissions from each chloride process line as required in this subpart.


(b) You must report CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O emissions from each stationary combustion unit under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) by following the requirements of subpart C.
