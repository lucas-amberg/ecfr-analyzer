# ยง 98.342   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report CH_4_ generation and CH_4_ emissions from landfills.


(b) You must report CH_4_ destruction resulting from landfill gas collection and combustion systems.


(c) You must report under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) the emissions of CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O from each stationary combustion unit following the requirements of subpart C.
