# ยง 98.152   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) the emissions of CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O from each stationary combustion unit following the requirements of subpart C.


(b) You must report HFC-23 emissions from HCFC-22 production processes and HFC-23 destruction processes.
