# ยง 98.192   GHGs to report.

You must report:


(a) CO_2_ process emissions from lime kilns.


(b) CO_2_ emissions from fuel combustion at lime kilns.


(c) N_2_O and CH_4_ emissions from fuel combustion at each lime kiln. You must report these emissions under 40 CFR part 98, subpart C (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources).


(d) CO_2_, N_2_O, and CH_4_ emissions from each stationary fuel combustion unit other than lime kilns. You must report these emissions under 40 CFR part 98, subpart C (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources).


(e) CO_2_ collected and transferred off site under 40 CFR part 98, following the requirements of subpart PP of this part (Suppliers of Carbon Dioxide (CO_2_)).
