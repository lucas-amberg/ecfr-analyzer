# ยง 98.462   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report CH_4_ generation and CH_4_ emissions from industrial waste landfills.


(b) You must report CH_4_ destruction resulting from landfill gas collection and destruction devices, if present.


(c) You must report under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) the emissions of CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O from each stationary combustion unit associated with the landfill gas destruction device, if present, by following the requirements of subpart C of this part.
