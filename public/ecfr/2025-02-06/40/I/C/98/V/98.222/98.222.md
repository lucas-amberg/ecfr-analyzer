# ยง 98.222   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report N_2_O process emissions from each nitric acid train as required by this subpart.


(b) You must report under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) the emissions of CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O from each stationary combustion unit by following the requirements of subpart C.
