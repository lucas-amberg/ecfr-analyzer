# ยง 98.262   GHGs to report.

(a) You must report CO_2_ process emissions from each wet-process phosphoric acid process line.


(b) You must report under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) the emissions of CO_2_, CH_4_, and N_2_O from each stationary combustion unit following the requirements of subpart C of this part.
