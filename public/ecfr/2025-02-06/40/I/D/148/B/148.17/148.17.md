(a) Effective November 9, 1992, the wastes specified in 40 CFR part 261 as EPA hazardous waste numbers F037, F038, K107, K108, K109, K110, K111, K112, K117, K118, K123, K124, K125, K126, K131, K136, U328, U353, and U359 are prohibited from underground injection.

(c) [Reserved]


(d) Effective June 30, 1995, the wastes specified in 40 CFR part 261 as EPA Hazardous waste numbers K117, K118, K131, and K132 are prohibited from underground injection. 


(e) The requirements of paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section do not apply: 


(1) If the wastes meet or are treated to meet the applicable standards specified in subpart D of part 268; or 


(2) If an exemption from a prohibition has been granted in response to a petition under subpart C of this part; or
