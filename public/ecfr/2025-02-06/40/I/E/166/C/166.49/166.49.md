# ยง 166.49   Public notice of crisis exemptions.

(a) *Periodic notices.* At least quarterly, the Administrator shall issue a notice in the _Federal Register_ announcing issuance of crisis exemptions. The notice shall contain all of the following:


(1) The name of the applicant;


(2) The pesticide authorized for use;


(3) The crop or site to be treated; and


(4) The name, address, and telephone number of a person in the Agency who can provide further information. 


(b) *Annual reports.* Annually, the Agency shall issue a notice in the _Federal Register_ that shall summarize:


(1) The number of crisis exemptions declared; and


(2) The number of crisis exemptions revoked.
