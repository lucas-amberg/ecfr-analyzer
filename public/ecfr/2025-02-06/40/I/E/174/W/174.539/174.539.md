# ยง 174.539   Cry51Aa2.834__16 protein; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.

Residues of the Cry51Aa2.834__16 protein, which is a modified protein derived from the Cry51Aa2 protein of *Bacillus thuringiensis,* in or on cotton are exempt from the requirement of a tolerance, when the Cry51Aa2.834__16 protein is used as a plant-incorporated protectant.
