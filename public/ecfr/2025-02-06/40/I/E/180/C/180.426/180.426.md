# ยง 180.426   2-[4,5-Dihydro-4-methyl-4-(1-methylethyl)-5-oxo-1

A tolerance is established for residues of the herbicide 2-[4,5-dihydro-4-methyl-4-(1-methylethyl)-5-oxo-1*H*-imidazol-2-yl]-3-quinoline carboxylic acid, in or on the raw agricultural commodity soybean at 0.05 part per million.
