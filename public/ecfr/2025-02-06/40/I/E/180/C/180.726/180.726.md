(b) *Section 18 emergency exemptions.* Time-limited tolerances are established for residues of the herbicide metamitron, including its metabolites and degradates, in or on the specified agricultural commodities to table 1 to this paragraph (b), resulting from use of the pesticide pursuant to FIFRA section 18 emergency exemptions. Compliance with the tolerance levels specified in table 1 to this paragraph (b) is to be determined by measuring residues of metamitron (4-amino-3-methyl-6-phenyl-1,2,4-triazin-5(4*H*)-one) in or on the listed commodities. The tolerances expire on the dates specified in table 1 to this paragraph (b).
