# ยง 180.1191   Ferric phosphate; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of the biochemical pesticide, ferric phosphate (FePO_4_, CAS No. 11045-86-0) in or on all food commodities.
