# ยง 180.1284   Ammonium salts of higher fatty acids (C

Ammonium salts of C_8_-C_18_ saturated and C_8_-C_12_ unsaturated higher fatty acids are exempted from the requirement of a tolerance for residues in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with good agricultural practice.
