# ยง 180.1305   Chromobacterium subtsugae strain PRAA4-1


An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of *Chromobacterium subtsugae* strain PRAA4-1
T in or on all food commodities when applied as an insecticide or miticide and used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
