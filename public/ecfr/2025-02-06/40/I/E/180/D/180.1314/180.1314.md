# ยง 180.1314   Killed, nonviable 

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of killed, nonviable *Streptomyces acidiscabies* strain RL-110
T in or on all food commodities when applied as a pre- or post-emergent herbicide and used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
