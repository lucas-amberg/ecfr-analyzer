# ยง 180.1342   

(a) An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of *d*-limonene, (4*R*)-1-methyl-4-(1-methylethenyl)cyclohexene, in or on all food commodities when applied as an herbicide in accordance with good agricultural practices.


(b) An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of *d*-limonene, (4*R*)-1-methyl-4-(1-methylethenyl)cyclohexene, in or on all food commodities when applied as an insecticide in kitchens and pantries.
