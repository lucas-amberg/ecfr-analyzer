# § 410.61   Specialized definitions.

In addition to the definitions set forth in 40 CFR part 401 and § 410.01 of this part, the following definitions apply to this subpart:


(a) The term *product* shall mean the final carpet produced or processed including the primary backing but excluding the secondary backing.


(b) The term *simple manufacturing operation* shall mean the following unit processes: fiber preparation and dyeing with or without carpet backing.


(c) The term *complex manufacturing operation* shall mean “simple” unit processes (fiber preparation, dyeing and carpet backing) plus any additional manufacturing operations such as printing or dyeing and printing.
