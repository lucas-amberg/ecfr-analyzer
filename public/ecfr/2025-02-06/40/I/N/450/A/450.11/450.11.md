# ยง 450.11   General definitions.

(a) *New source.* New source means any source, whose discharges are defined in 40 CFR 122.26(b)(14)(x) and (b)(15), that commences construction activity after the effective date of this rule.


(b) *Infeasible.* Infeasible means not technologically possible, or not economically practicable and achievable in light of best industry practices.
