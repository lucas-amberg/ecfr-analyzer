# ยง 751.303   Definitions.

The definitions in subpart A of this part apply to this subpart unless otherwise specified in this section. In addition, the following definitions apply:


*Distribute in commerce* has the same meaning as in section 3 of the Act, except that the term does not include retailers for purposes of ยง 751.321 and ยง 751.323.


*Interim ECEL* means a concentration of airborne TCE of 0.2 parts per million (ppm) calculated as an eight (8)-hour time weighted average (TWA) that will be in place only for the timeframes indicated for specified conditions of use, after which prohibitions would take effect.


*Interim ECEL action level* means a concentration of airborne TCE of 0.1 parts per million (ppm) calculated as an eight (8)-hour time-weighted average (TWA).


*Site-limited* has the same meaning as in 40 CFR 711.3.
