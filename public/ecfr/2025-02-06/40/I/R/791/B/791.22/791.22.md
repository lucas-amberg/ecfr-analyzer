# ยง 791.22   Consolidation of hearings.

(a) Promptly upon receipt of the request for a hearing, the Administrator will publish a notice in the _Federal Register,_ advising those subject to the test rule that a request for a hearing has been made.


(b) Any other person wishing to participate in the hearing shall so notify EPA within 45 days of the _Federal Register_ notice. EPA will promptly inform the regional office of the American Arbitration Association where the request has been filed of the additional parties.
