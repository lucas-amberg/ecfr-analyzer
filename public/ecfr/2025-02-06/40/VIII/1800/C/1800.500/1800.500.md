# § 1800.500   Allocation.

Using the data from §§ 1800.200 through 1800.402 of this subpart in the formula provided in § 1800.101 of this subpart yields the following allocation for each Gulf Coast State:


(a) Alabama—20.40%.


(b) Florida—18.36%.


(c) Louisiana—34.59%.


(d) Mississippi—19.07%.


(e) Texas—7.58%.
