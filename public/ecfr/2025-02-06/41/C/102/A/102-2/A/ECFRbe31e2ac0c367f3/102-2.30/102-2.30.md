# § 102-2.30   Where and in what formats is the FMR published?

Proposed rules are published in the _Federal Register._ FMR bulletins are published in looseleaf format. FMR interim and final rules are published in the following formats—


(a) _Federal Register_ under the “Rules and Regulations” section.


(b) Loose-leaf. (See § 102-2.35.)


(c) Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), which is an annual codification of the general and permanent rules published in the _Federal Register._ The CFR is available on line and in a bound-volume format.


(d) Electronically on the Internet.
