# § 301-11.29   Are lodging facilities required to accept a generic federal, state or local tax exempt certificate?

Exemptions from taxes for Federal travelers, and the forms required to claim them, vary from location to location. The GSA SmartPay ® Program Support office provides more information regarding state tax exemptions on its Web site (*https://smartpay.gsa.gov/content/state-tax-information*) and by e-mail (*gsa_smartpay@gsa.gov*).
