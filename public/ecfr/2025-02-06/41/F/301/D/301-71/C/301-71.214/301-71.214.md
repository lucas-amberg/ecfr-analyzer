# ยง 301-71.214   Does mandatory use of the Government contractor-issued travel charge card change the employee's obligation to pay their travel card bill by the due date?

No, mandatory use of the Government contractor-issued travel charge card does not relieve the employee of their obligation to honor their cardholder payment agreement.
