# ยง 301-76.102   Can we collect undisputed delinquent amounts if we have not reimbursed the employee for amounts reimbursable under applicable travel regulations?

No, you may only collect undisputed delinquent amounts after you have reimbursed the employee under the applicable travel regulations and in accordance with a proper travel claim. However, if the employee has not submitted a proper travel claim within the timeframe requirements of ยง 301-52.7 of this chapter, and there are no extenuating circumstances, you may collect the undisputed delinquent amounts.
