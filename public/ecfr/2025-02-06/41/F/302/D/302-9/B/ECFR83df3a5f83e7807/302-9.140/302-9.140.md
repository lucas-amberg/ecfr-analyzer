# ยง 302-9.140   Under what specific conditions may my agency authorize transportation of a POV to my post of duty upon my assignment to that post of duty?

Your agency may authorize transportation of a POV to your post of duty when: 


(a) It has determined in accordance with ยง 302-9.604 that it is in the interest of the Government for you to have use of your POV at the post of duty; 


(b) You have signed a service agreement; and 


(c) You meet any specific conditions your agency has established.
