# ยง 7.5   Payment procedures.

An up-to-date fee schedule and instructions for terms of payment are available from the Division of Scientific Resources, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, 1600 Clifton Road, MS C-17, Atlanta, Georgia 30333 or 404-639-3466. Any changes in the fee schedule will be published in the _Federal Register._ The fee must be paid in U.S. dollars at the time that the requester requests the biological reference standard or biological preparation.
