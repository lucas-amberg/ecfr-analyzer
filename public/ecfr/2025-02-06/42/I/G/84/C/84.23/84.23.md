# ยง 84.23   Fee revision.

(a) Each fee schedule will remain in effect for at least 2 years and will be revised as needed to reflect cost increases identified in biennial reviews.


(b) Fee schedule updates will be proposed in a notice of proposed rulemaking published in the _Federal Register_.


(c) The current fee schedules will be published in Appendix A and Appendix B of this part and will remain in effect until the effective date of the new fee schedules published in the _Federal Register_.
