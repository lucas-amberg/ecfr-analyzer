# ยง 84.191   Chemical cartridge respirators; required components.

(a) Each chemical cartridge respirator described in ยง 84.190 shall, where its design requires, contain the following component parts:


(1) Facepiece, mouthpiece, and noseclip, hood, or helmet;


(2) Cartridge;
