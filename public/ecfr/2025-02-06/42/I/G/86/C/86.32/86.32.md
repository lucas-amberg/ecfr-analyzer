# ยง 86.32   Application for direct traineeship.

An application for a direct traineeship under this subpart shall be submitted to the Secretary at such times and in such form and manner as he may prescribe. 
1 In addition to the information supplied by the applicant in his application, such further information may be required as is necessary to determine his or her qualifications.
