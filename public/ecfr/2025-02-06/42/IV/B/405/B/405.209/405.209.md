# ยง 405.209   Payment for a Category B (Nonexperimental/investigational) device.

Payment under Medicare for a Category B (Nonexperimental/investigational) device is based on, and may not exceed, the amount that would have been paid for a currently used device serving the same medical purpose that has been approved or cleared for marketing by the FDA.
