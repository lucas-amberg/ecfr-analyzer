# ยง 405.1130   Effect of the Council's decision.

The Council's decision is final and binding on all parties unless a Federal district court issues a decision modifying the Council's decision or the decision is revised as the result of a reopening in accordance with ยง 405.980. A party may file an action in a Federal district court within 60 calendar days after the date it receives notice of the Council's decision.
