# ยง 410.172   Payment for partial hospitalization services in CMHCs: Conditions.

Medicare Part B pays for partial hospitalization services furnished in a CMHC on behalf of an individual only if the following conditions are met: 


(a) The CMHC files a written request for payment on the CMS form 1450 and in the manner prescribed by CMS; and 


(b) The services are furnished in accordance with the requirements described in ยง 410.110.
