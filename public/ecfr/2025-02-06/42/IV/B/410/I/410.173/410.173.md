# ยง 410.173   Payment for intensive outpatient services in CMHCs: Conditions.

Medicare Part B pays for intensive outpatient services furnished in a CMHC on behalf of an individual only if the following conditions are met:


(a) The CMHC files a written request for payment on the CMS form 1450 and in the manner prescribed by CMS; and


(b) The services are furnished in accordance with the requirements described in ยง 410.111.
