# ยง 413.320   Publication of prospectively determined payment rates or amounts.

At least 90 days before the beginning of a Federal fiscal year to which revised prospectively determined payment rates are to be applied, CMS publishes a notice in the _Federal Register:_

(a) Establishing the prospectively determined payment rates for routine services; and 


(b) Explaining the basis on which the prospectively determined payment rates are calculated.
