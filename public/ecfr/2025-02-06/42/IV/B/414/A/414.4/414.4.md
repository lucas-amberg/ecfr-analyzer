# ยง 414.4   Fee schedule areas.

(a) *General.* CMS establishes physician fee schedule areas that generally conform to the geographic localities in existence before January 1, 1992.


(b) *Changes.* CMS announces proposed changes to fee schedule areas in the _Federal Register_ and provides an opportunity for public comment. After considering public comments, CMS publishes the final changes in the _Federal Register._
