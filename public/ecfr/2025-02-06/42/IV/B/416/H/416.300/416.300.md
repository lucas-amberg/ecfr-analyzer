# ยง 416.300   Basis and scope of subpart.

(a) *Statutory basis.* Section 1833(i)(2)(D)(iv) and (i)(7) of the Act authorizes the Secretary to implement a revised ASC payment system in a manner so as to provide for a 2.0 percentage point reduction in any annual update for an ASC's failure to report on quality measures in accordance with the Secretary's requirements.


(b) *Scope.* This subpart contains specific requirements and standards for the ASCQR Program.
