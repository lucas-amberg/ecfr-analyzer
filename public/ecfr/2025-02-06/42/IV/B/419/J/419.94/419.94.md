# ยง 419.94   Preclusion of administrative and judicial review.

There is no administrative or judicial review under section 1869 of the Act, section 1878 of the Act, or otherwise of the following:


(a) The determination of whether a rural emergency hospital meets the requirements of this subpart.


(b) The determination of payment amounts under this subpart.


(c) The requirements established by this subpart.
