# ยง 423.2500   Basis and scope.

(a) *Basis.* This subpart is based on section 1860D-14 of the Social Security Act.


(b) *Scope.* This subpart sets forth the requirements for the Limited Income Newly Eligible Transition (LI NET) program that begins no later than January 1, 2024. Under this program, eligible individuals are provided transitional coverage for Part D drugs.
