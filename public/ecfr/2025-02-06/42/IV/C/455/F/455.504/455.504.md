# § 455.504   Definitions.

As used in this subpart—


*Medicaid RAC program* means a recovery audit contractor program administered by a State to identify overpayments and underpayments and recoup overpayments.


*Medicare RAC program* means a recovery audit contractor program administered by CMS to identify underpayments and overpayments and recoup overpayments, established under the authority of section 1893(h) of the Act.
