# § 456.126   Time limits for final decision and notification of adverse decision.

Except as required under § 456.127, the UR plan must provide that the committee makes a final decision on a beneficiary's need for admission and gives notice of an adverse final decision—


(a) Within two working days after admission, for an individual who is receiving Medicaid at that time; or 


(b) Within two working days after the hospital is notified of the application for Medicaid, for an individual who applies while in the hospital.
