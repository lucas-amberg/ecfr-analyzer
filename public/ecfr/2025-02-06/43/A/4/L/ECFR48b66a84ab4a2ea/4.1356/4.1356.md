# ยง 4.1356   Appeals.

(a) Any party aggrieved by the decision of the administrative law judge may appeal to the Board under procedures set forth in ยง 4.1271 *et seq.* of this subpart, except that the notice of appeal must be filed within 20 days of receipt of the administrative law judge's decision.
