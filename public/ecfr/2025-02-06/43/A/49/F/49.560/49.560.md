(a) The final assessment of civil penalty if the person served with the final assessment does not file a timely request for hearing under ยง 49.535(a)(2).


(b) The administrative law judge's decision on the request for hearing if a timely appeal to the OHA Director is not filed under ยง 49.550(a); or
