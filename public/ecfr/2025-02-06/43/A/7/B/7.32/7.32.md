# ยง 7.32   Supplemental definitions.

For purposes of this subpart, the following definitions will be used:


(a) *Site of religious or cultural importance* means, for purposes of ยง 7.7 of this part, a location which has traditionally been considered important by an Indian tribe because of a religious event which happened there; because it contains specific natural products which are of religious or cultural importance; because it is believed to the be dwelling place of, the embodiment of, or a place conducive to communication with spiritual beings; because it contains elements of life-cycle rituals, such as burials and associated materials; or because it has other specific and continuing significance in Indian religion or culture.


(b) *Allotted lands* means lands granted to Indian individuals by the United States and held in trust for those individuals by the United States.
