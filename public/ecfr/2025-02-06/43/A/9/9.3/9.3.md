# ยง 9.3   What programs and activities of the Department are subject to these regulations?

(a) The Secretary publishes in the _Federal Register_ a list of the Department's programs and activities that are subject to these regulations and a list of programs and activities that have existing consultation processes.


(b) With respect to programs and activities that a state chooses to cover, and that have existing consultation processes, the state must agree to adopt those existing processes.
