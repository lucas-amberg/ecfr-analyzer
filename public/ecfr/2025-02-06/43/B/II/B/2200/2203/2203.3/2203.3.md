# ยง 2203.3   Public meeting.

Upon completion of an environmental analysis, but prior to the issuance of a notice of decision, the authorized officer shall publish a notice in the _Federal Register_ setting a time and place where a public meeting will be held to receive public comment on the public interest factors of the proposed exchange. Such notice shall be distributed in accordance with ยง 2201.7-1 of this title. The public meeting shall: 


(a) Follow procedures established by the authorized officer, which shall be announced prior to the meeting; and 


(b) Be recorded and a transcript prepared, with the transcript and all written submissions being made a part of the public record of the proposed exchange.
