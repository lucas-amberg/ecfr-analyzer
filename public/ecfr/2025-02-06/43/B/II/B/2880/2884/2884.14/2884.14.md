# ยง 2884.14   When does the BLM reevaluate the cost recovery fees?

BLM reevaluates the processing and monitoring fees (*see* ยง 2885.23 of this part) for each category and the categories themselves within 5 years after they go into effect and at 10-year intervals after that. When reevaluating processing and monitoring fees, BLM considers all factors that affect the fees, including, but not limited to, any changes in:


(a) Technology;


(b) The procedures for processing applications and monitoring grants;


(c) Statutes and regulations relating to the right-of-way program; or


(d) The IPD-GDP.
