# § 3179.30   Incorporation by Reference (IBR).

Certain material is incorporated by reference into this subpart with the approval of the Director of the Federal Register under 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51. To enforce any edition other than that specified in this section, the BLM must publish a rule in the _Federal Register,_ and the material must be reasonably available to the public. All approved incorporation by reference (IBR) material is available for inspection at the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) and at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA).
