# ยง 3505.12   How do I obtain a prospecting permit?

Deliver 3 copies of the BLM application form to the BLM office with jurisdiction over the lands you are interested in. Include the first year's rental with your application. You will also be charged a processing fee, which BLM will determine on a case-by-case basis as described in ยง 3000.11 of this chapter. For more information on fees and rentals, see subpart 3504 of this part.
