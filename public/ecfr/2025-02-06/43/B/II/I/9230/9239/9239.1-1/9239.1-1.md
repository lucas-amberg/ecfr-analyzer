# § 9239.1-1   Unauthorized cutting, removal, or injury.

(a) All of the definitions in § 5400.0-5 of this title apply to this section.


(b) Commission of any of the acts listed in §§ 5462.2 and 5511.4 of this title constitutes a trespass.
