# ยง 98.17   Period covered by Plan.

(a) For States, Territories, and Indian Tribes the Plan shall cover a period of three years.


(b) The Lead Agency shall submit a new Plan prior to the expiration of the time period specified in paragraph (a) of this section, at such time as required by the Secretary in written instructions.
