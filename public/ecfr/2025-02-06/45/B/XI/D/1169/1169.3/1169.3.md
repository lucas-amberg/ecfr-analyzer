# ยง 1169.3   Inquiries about NEH's systems of records or implementation of the Privacy Act.

Inquiries about NEH's systems of records or implementation of the Privacy Act should be sent by email to *gencounsel@neh.gov* or by mail to the following address: National Endowment for the Humanities, Office of the General Counsel, 400 Seventh Street SW, Room 4060, Washington, DC 20506.
