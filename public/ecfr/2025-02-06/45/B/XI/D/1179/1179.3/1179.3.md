# ยง 1179.3   Applicability.

(a) These regulations are to be followed when:


(1) The National Endowment for the Humanities is owed a debt by an individual currently employed by another Federal agency;


(2) The National Endowment for the Humanities is owed a debt by an individual who is a current employee of the National Endowment for the Humanities; or


(3) The National Endowment for the Humanities employs an individual who owes a debt to another Federal agency.
