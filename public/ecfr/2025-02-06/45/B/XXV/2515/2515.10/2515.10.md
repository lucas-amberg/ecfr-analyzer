# ยง 2515.10   What are the service-learning programs of the Corporation for National and Community Service?

(a) There are three service-learning programs: (1) School-based programs, described in part 2516 of this chapter.


(2) Community-based programs, described in part 2517 of this chapter.


(3) Higher education programs, described in part 2519 of this chapter.


(b) Each program gives participants the opportunity to learn and develop their own capabilities through service-learning, while addressing needs in the community.
