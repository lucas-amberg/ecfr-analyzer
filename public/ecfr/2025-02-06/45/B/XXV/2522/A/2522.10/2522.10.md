# ยง 2522.10   What definitions apply to this part?

*AmeriCorps* means the Corporation for National and Community Service, established pursuant to section 191 of the National and Community Service Act of 1990, as amended, 42 U.S.C. 12651, which operates as AmeriCorps.


*You.* For this part, *you* refers to the grantee, unless otherwise noted.
