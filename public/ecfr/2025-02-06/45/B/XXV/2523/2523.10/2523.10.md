# ยง 2523.10   Are Federal agencies eligible to apply for AmeriCorps program funds?

Yes. Federal agencies may apply for and receive AmeriCorps funds under parts 2521 and 2522 of this chapter, and they are eligible to receive up to one-third of the funds available for competitive distribution under ยง 2521.30(b)(3) of this chapter. The Corporation may transfer funds available to it to other Federal agencies.
