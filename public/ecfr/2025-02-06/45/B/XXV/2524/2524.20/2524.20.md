# ยง 2524.20   What are the guidelines for program development assistance and training grants?

(a) *Eligibility.* States, Federal agencies, Indian tribes, public or private nonprofit agencies, institutions of higher education, for-profit businesses, and individuals may apply for assistance under this section. 


(b) *Duration.* A grant made under this section will be for a term of up to one year and is renewable. 


(c) *Application requirements.* Eligible applicants must comply with the requirements specified in the Corporation's application package.
