# ยง 2525.440   Is there a limit on the number of recipients an individual may designate to receive a transferred award?

(a) An Eligible Individual may transfer all or part of a non-expired education award to no more than two recipients.


(b) If a Designated Recipient rejects, in whole or in part, a transferred education award, or a transfer was revoked in accordance with ยง 2525.480, the education award can be transferred to another Designated Recipient, so long as the education award has not yet expired.
