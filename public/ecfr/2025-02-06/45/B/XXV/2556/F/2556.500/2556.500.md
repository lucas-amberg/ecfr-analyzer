# ยง 2556.500   How is a position for a summer associate established in a project?

Subject to VISTA assistance availability, AmeriCorps approves the establishment of summer associate positions based on the following factors:


(a) The need in the community, as demonstrated by the sponsor, for the performance of project activities by a summer associate(s);


(b) The content and quality of summer associate project plans;


(c) The capacity of the sponsor to implement the summer associate project activities; and


(d) The sponsor's compliance with all applicable parts of the DVSA, VISTA program policy, and the sponsor's Memorandum of Agreement, which incorporates their project application.
