# ยง 42.05-63   Ship(s) and vessel(s).

The terms *ship*(*s*) and *vessel*(*s*) are interchangeable or synonymous words, and include every description of watercraft, other than a seaplane on the water, used or capable of being used as a means of transportation on water.
