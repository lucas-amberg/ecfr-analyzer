# § 63.15-1   General.

(a) Each automatic auxiliary boiler must be designed and constructed for its intended service according to the requirements of the parts referenced in table 1 to § 54.01-5 of this subchapter.


(b) Controls and safety devices for automatic auxiliary boilers must meet the applicable requirements of ASME CSD-1 (incorporated by reference, see § 63.05-1), except Paragraph CG-310.


(c) All devices and components of an automatic auxiliary boiler must satisfactorily operate within the marine environment. The boiler must satisfactorily operate with a momentary roll of 30°, a list of 15°, and a permanent trim of 5° with it installed in a position as specified by the manufacturer.


(d) An electrical control used to shut down the automatic auxiliary boiler must be installed in accordance with § 58.01-25 of this subchapter. This device must stop the fuel supply to the fuel burning equipment.


(e) Mercury tube actuated controls are prohibited from being installed and used on automatic auxiliary boilers.
