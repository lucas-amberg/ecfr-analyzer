# § 64.17   Minimum tank thickness.

(a) Except as allowed in paragraph (b) of this section, a tank with a diameter of—


(1) 6 feet or less must have a shell and head of 
3/16 inch thickness or more; or 


(2) More than 6 feet must have a shell and head of 
1/4 inch thickness or more. 


(b) If the tank has additional framework to guard against accidental puncturing of the tank, the shell and head thickness must be 
1/8 inch or more.
