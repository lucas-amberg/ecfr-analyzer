# § 64.77   Inspection and test.

For the handling and stowage requirements in § 98.30-3 of this chapter, each MPT must pass the following inspections and tests conducted by the owner or the owner's representative:


(a) Pressure relief and vacuum relief devices must be inspected one time or more during each 12 month period of service in accordance with § 64.79. 


(b) An MPT must be inspected during the 30 months before any month in which it is in service in accordance with § 64.81. 


(c) An MPT must pass a hydrostatic test in accordance with § 64.83 during the 60 months before any month in which it is in service. 


(d) After each welded repair, an MPT must pass a hydrostatic test in accordance with § 64.83.
