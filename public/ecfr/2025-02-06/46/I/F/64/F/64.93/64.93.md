# § 64.93   Pump controls.

(a) A pressure gauge must be installed—


(1) On the pump discharge; 


(2) Near the pump controls; and 


(3) Visible to the operator. 


(b) A pump must have a remote, quick acting, manual shutdown that is conspicuously labeled and located in an easily accessible area away from the pump. The quick acting, manual shutdown for remote operation must provide a means of stopping the pump power unit.
