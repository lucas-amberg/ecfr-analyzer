# ยง 67.259   Requirements for amendments to notice of claim of lien.

An amendment to notice of claim of lien presented for filing and recording must:


(a) Be signed by or on behalf of each original claimant or last assignee of record; and


(b) Recite the nature of the change being effected by the instrument.
