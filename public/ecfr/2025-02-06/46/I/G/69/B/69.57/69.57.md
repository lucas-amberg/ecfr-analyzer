# ยง 69.57   Gross tonnage ITC.

Gross tonnage ITC (GT ITC) is determined by the following formula GT ITC = K_1_ V, in which V = total volume of all enclosed spaces in cubic meters and K_1_ = 0.2 + 0.02 log_10_ V.
