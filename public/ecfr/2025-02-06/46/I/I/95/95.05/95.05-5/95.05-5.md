# ยง 95.05-5   Fire main system.

(a) Fire pumps, hydrants, hose, and nozzles shall be installed on the following vessels: 


(1) On all self-propelled vessels. 


(2) On all barges with sleeping accommodations for more than 12 persons. 


(b) The arrangements and details of the fire main system shall be as set forth in subpart 95.10.
