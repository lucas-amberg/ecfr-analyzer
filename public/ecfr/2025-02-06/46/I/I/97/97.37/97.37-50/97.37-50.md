# § 97.37-50   Ventilation alarm failure.

(a) The alarm required by § 92.15-10(d)(4) of this subchapter, which indicates the loss of required ventilation in spaces specially suitable for vehicles, shall be marked with a conspicuous sign in at least 
1/4-inch letters “VENTILATION FAILURE IN VEHICULAR SPACE.”
