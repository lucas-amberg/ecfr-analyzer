# § 98.30-31   Conditions for pumping.

No person may start pumping a product to or from a portable tank or IBC or if started, continue to pump if—


(a) There is an electrical storm; 


(b) A fire occurs—


(1) On the deck; 


(2) On the vessel; 


(3) In the vicinity; or 


(c) The cargo hose ruptures or leaks.
