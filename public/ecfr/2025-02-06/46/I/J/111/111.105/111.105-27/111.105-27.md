# ยง 111.105-27   Belt drives.

Each belt drive in a hazardous location must have:


(a) A conductive belt; and


(b) Pulleys, shafts, and driving equipment grounded to meet NFPA 77 (incorporated by reference, see 46 CFR 110.10-1).
