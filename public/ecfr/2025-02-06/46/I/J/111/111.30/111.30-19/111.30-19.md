# § 111.30-19   Buses and wiring.

(a) *General.* Each bus must meet the requirements of either—




(1) Section 5.10 of IEEE 45.7-2012 (incorporated by reference; see § 110.10-1 of this subchapter); or




(2) IEC 60092-302:1997 (clause 7) (incorporated by reference; see § 110.10-1 of this subchapter).


(b) *Wiring.* Instrumentation and control wiring must be—


(1) Suitable for installation within in a switchboard enclosure and be rated at 90 °C or higher;


(2) Stranded copper;


(3) No. 14 AWG (2.10 mm
2) or larger or must be ribbon cable or similar conductor size cable recommended for use in low-power instrumentation, monitoring, or control circuits by the equipment manufacturer;


(4) Flame-retardant meeting test VW-1 of ANSI/UL 2556 or IEC 60332-1-1:2015 and IEC 60332-1-2:2015 (all incorporated by reference; see § 110.10-1 of this subchapter); and




(5) Extra flexible, if used on a hinged panel.
