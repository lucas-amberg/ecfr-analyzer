# § 111.33-5   Installation.

Each semiconductor converter system must meet the installation requirements, as appropriate, of—


(a) Sections 4.31.19.2, 4.31.19.7, and 4.31.19.8 of IEEE 45.2-2011 (incorporated by reference; see § 110.10-1 of this subchapter); or


(b) IEC 60092-304:1980 (incorporated by reference; see § 110.10-1 of this subchapter).
