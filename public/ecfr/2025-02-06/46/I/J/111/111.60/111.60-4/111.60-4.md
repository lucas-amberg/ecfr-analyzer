# § 111.60-4   Minimum cable conductor size.

Each cable conductor must be 18 AWG (0.82 mm
2) or larger except—


(a) Each power and lighting cable conductor must be 14 AWG (2.10 mm
2) or larger; and


(b) Each thermocouple, pyrometer, or instrumentation cable conductor must be 22 AWG (0.33 mm
2) or larger.
