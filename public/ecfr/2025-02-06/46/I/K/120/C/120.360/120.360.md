# § 120.360   Semiconductor rectifier systems.

(a) Each semiconductor rectifier system must have an adequate heat removal system that prevents overheating.


(b) Where a semiconductor rectifier system is used in a propulsion system or in other vital systems it must:


(1) Have a current limiting circuit;


(2) Have external overcurrent protection; and


(3) Meet Sections 35.84.2 and 35.84.4 of the American Bureau of Shipping (ABS), “Rules for Building and Classing Steel Vessels,” or other standard specified by the Commandant.
