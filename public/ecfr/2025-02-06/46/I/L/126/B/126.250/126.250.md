# ยง 126.250   Period of validity for a Certificate of Inspection.

(a) A Certificate of Inspection is valid for 5 years. 


(b) A Certificate of Inspection may be suspended and withdrawn or revoked by the cognizant OCMI at any time for noncompliance with the requirements of this subchapter or other applicable laws.
