# ยง 150.160   Carrying a cargo as an exception to the compatibility chart.

The Operator of a vessel having on board a cargo carried as an exception under ยง 150.150 but not listed in Appendix I, Exceptions to the Chart, shall make sure that:


(a) The Commandant (CG-ENG-5) has authorized by letter or message the cargo pair as an exception to the compatibility chart; and
