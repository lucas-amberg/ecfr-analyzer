# ยง 153.920   Cargo quantity limitations.

(a) No person may load a cargo tank or operate a tankship that carries a cargo tank containing in excess of 1250 m
3 (approx. 44,138 ft
3) of cargo requiring a type I containment system. 


(b) No person may load a cargo tank or operate a tankship that carries a cargo tank containing in excess of 3000 m
3 (approx. 105,932 ft
3) of a cargo requiring a type II containment system.
