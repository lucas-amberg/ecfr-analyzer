# ยง 154.419   Design vapor pressure.

The P_o_ of an integral tank must not exceed 24.5 kPa gauge (3.55 psig) unless special approval by the Commandant (CG-ENG) allows a P_o_ between 24.5 kPa gauge (3.55 psig) and 69 kPa gauge (10 psig).
