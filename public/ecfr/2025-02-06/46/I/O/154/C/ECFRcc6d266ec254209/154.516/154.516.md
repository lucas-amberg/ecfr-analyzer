# ยง 154.516   Piping: Hull protection.

A vessel's hull must be protected from low temperature liquid leakage by a drip pan, or other means specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG), at:


(a) Each piping connection dismantled on a routine basis;


(b) Cargo discharge and loading manifolds; and


(c) Pump seals.
