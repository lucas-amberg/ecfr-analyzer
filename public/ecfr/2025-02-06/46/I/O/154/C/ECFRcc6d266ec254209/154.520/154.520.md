# § 154.520   Piping calculations.

A piping system must be designed to meet the allowable stress values under § 56.07-10 of this chapter and, if the design temperature is −110 °C (−166 °F) or lower, the stress analysis must be specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG) and must include: 


(a) Pipe weight loads; 


(b) Acceleration loads; 


(c) Internal pressure loads; 


(d) Thermal loads; and 


(e) Loads from the hull.
