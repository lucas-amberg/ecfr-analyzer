# ยง 154.438   Design vapor pressure.

(a) If the surface of an independent tank type A are mostly flat surfaces, the P_o_ must not exceed 69 kPa gauge (10 psig). 


(b) If the surfaces of an independent tank type A are formed by bodies of revolution, the design calculation of the P_o_ must be specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG).
