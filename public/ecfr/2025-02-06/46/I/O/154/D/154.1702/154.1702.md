# ยง 154.1702   Materials of construction.

When Table 4 references one of the following paragraphs in this section, the materials in the referenced paragraph must not be in components that contact the cargo liquid or vapor:


(a) Aluminum and aluminum bearing alloys.


(b) Copper and copper bearing alloys.


(c) Zinc or galvanized steel.


(d) Magnesium.


(e) Mercury.


(f) Acetylide forming materials, such as copper, silver, and mercury.
