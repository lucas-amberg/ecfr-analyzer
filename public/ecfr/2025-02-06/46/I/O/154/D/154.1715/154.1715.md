# § 154.1715   Moisture control.

When a vessel is carrying sulfur dioxide, the master shall ensure that:


(a) A cargo tank is dry before it is loaded with sulfur dioxide; and


(b) Air or inert gas admitted into a cargo tank carrying sulfur dioxide during discharging or tank breathing has a moisture content equal to or less than the moisture content of air with a dew point of −45 °C (−49 °F) at atmospheric pressure.
