# ยง 160.060-23   Procedure for approval of design or material change.

(a) The manufacturer must submit any proposed changes in design, material, or construction to *typeapproval@uscg.mil* for approval before changing PFD production methods.


(b) Only the Commandant or a designated representative may make determinations of equivalence of design, construction, and materials.
