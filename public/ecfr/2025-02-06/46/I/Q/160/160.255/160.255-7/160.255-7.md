# § 160.255-7   Design, construction, and performance of lifejackets.

(a) Each Level 100 lifejacket design must—


(1) Meet the requirements in ANSI/CAN/UL 12402-4 (incorporated by reference, see § 160.255-5) for a Level 100 device, and the requirements of this subpart; and


(2) For novel or unique designs, meet any additional requirements that the Commandant may prescribe.


(b) Lifejackets must be of first quality workmanship and must be free from any defects materially affecting their appearance or serviceability.


(c) Lifejackets must not provide means intended for fastening or securing the device to a boat.
