# ยง 160.276-21   PFD manuals.

(a) An owner's manual in accordance with Figure 7DV of ANSI/CAN/UL 12402-5 (incorporated by reference, see ยง 160.276-5), must be provided with each inflatable PFD sold or offered for sale. The text of each manual is reviewed with the application for approval.


(b) The Commandant may prescribe additional information in the manual for special purpose devices or unique or novel designs.


(c) Additional information, instructions, or illustrations may be included in the owner's manual if there is no contradiction to the required information.
