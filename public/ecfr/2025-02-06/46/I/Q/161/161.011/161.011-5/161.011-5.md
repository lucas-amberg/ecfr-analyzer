# § 161.011-5   Types.

EPIRBs are typed as follows:


(a) Category 1—EPIRBs are capable of floating free of a vessel and activating automatically if the vessel sinks.


(b) Category 2—EPIRBs are manually removed from the mounting bracket and activated.
