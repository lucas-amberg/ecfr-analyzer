(a) Vessels contracted for prior to March 1, 1968, must meet the following requirements:


(1) Except as specifically modified by this paragraph, the requirements of ยง 193.50-10 must be complied with insofar as the number and general type of equipment is concerned.


(2) Existing installations previously approved, but not meeting the applicable requirements of ยง 193.50-10, may be continued in service so long as they are maintained in good condition to the satisfaction of the Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection, and they are in general agreement with the degree of safety prescribed by Table 193.50-10(a) of this subpart. (3) All new equipment and installations must meet the applicable requirements in this subpart for new vessels.
