# ยง 385.7   Issuance.

The MarAd assistance regulations are issued in the Code of Federal Regulations as Part 385, Chapter II, of Title 46, Shipping, after publication in the _Federal Register._ Copies of the MarAd assistance regulations in the _Federal Register_ and Code of Federal Regulations may be purchased from the Superintendent of Documents, Government Printing Office, Washington, DC 20402.
