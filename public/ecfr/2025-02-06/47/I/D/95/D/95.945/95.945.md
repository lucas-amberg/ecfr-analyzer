# ยง 95.945   Remote control of a CBRS station.

This section sets forth the conditions under which a CBRS station may be operated by remote control, pursuant to the exception in ยง 95.345. Operation of a CBRS station using a hands-free or other type of cordless microphone or headset authorized under part 15 is not considered to be remote control.


(a) *Wireless remote control.* No person shall operate a CBRS station by wireless remote control.


(b) *Wired remote control.* Before operating an CBRS station by wired remote control, the operator must obtain specific approval from the FCC. To obtain FCC approval, the operator must explain why wired remote control is needed. *See* ยง 95.329 regarding contacting the FCC.
