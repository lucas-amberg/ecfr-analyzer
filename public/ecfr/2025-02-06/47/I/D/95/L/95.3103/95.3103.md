# § 95.3103   Definitions, OBUs.

*Dedicated Short-range Communications Services (DSRCS).* A service providing for data transfer between various mobile and roadside transmitting units for the purposes of improving traffic flow, highway safety and performing other intelligent transportation functions. *See* § 90.7 of this chapter for a more detailed definition.


*On-Board Unit (OBU).* OBUs are low-power devices on vehicles that transfer data to roadside units in the Dedicated Short-Range Communications Service (*see* §§ 90.371-90.383 of this chapter), to improve traffic flow and safety, and for other intelligent transportation system purposes. *See* § 90.7 of this chapter.


*Roadside Unit (RSU). See* § 90.7 of this chapter.
