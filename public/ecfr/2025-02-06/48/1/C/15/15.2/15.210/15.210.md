# 15.210   Forms.

Prescribed forms are not required to prepare solicitations described in this part. The following forms may be used at the discretion of the contracting officer: 


(a) Standard Form 33, Solicitation, Offer, and Award, and Optional Form 308, Solicitation and Offerâ€”Negotiated Acquisition, may be used to issue RFPs and RFIs.
