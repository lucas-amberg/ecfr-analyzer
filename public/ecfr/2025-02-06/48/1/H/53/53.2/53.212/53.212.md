# 53.212   Acquisition of commercial products and commercial services.

*SF 1449 (Rev. NOV 2021), Solicitation/Contract/Order for Commercial Products and Commercial Services.* SF 1449 is prescribed for use in solicitations and contracts for commercial products and commercial services. Agencies may prescribe additional detailed instructions for use of the form.
