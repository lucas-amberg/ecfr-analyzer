# 1201.105-3   Copies.

(a) Copies of the TAR as published in _Federal Register_ and as set forth in the CFR may be purchased from the Government Publishing Office (GPO), U.S. Government Online Bookstore on the internet at *https://bookstore.gpo.gov/.*

(b) The TAR and Transportation Acquisition Circulars (TACs) are available on the internet at *https://www.acquisition.gov.*
