# 1316.307   Contract clauses.

(a) Insert a clause that is substantially the same as 1352.216-70, *Estimated and Allowable Costs,* in all cost-reimbursement contracts.


(b) Insert a clause similar to 1352.216-71, *Level of Effort (Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee, Term Contract),* in Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee, Level of Effort contracts.
