# 2901.105-3   Copies.

Copies of the DOLAR published in the _Federal Register_ or the CFR may be purchased from the Superintendent of Documents, Government Printing Office, Washington, DC 20402. Requests should reference the DOLAR as chapter 29 of title 48. The DOLAR is also available electronically at the Government Printing Office web page, *https://www.ecfr.gov/.* The CFR is printed in paperback edition with updates as needed.
