# 3001.105-3   Copies.

Official versions of the HSAR are available in the Code of Federal Regulations, as supplemented and revised from time to time by the _Federal Register,_ both of which are available from the Government Publishing Office in paper form. The HSAR is also available in electronic form at *https://www.ecfr.gov/.* The Homeland Security Acquisition Manual (HSAM), which complements the HSAR, can also be found at *http://www.dhs.gov.*
