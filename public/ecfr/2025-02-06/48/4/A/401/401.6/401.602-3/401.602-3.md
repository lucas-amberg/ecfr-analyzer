# 401.602-3   Ratification of unauthorized commitments.

(a) *Ratification* means the signed, documented action taken by an authorized official to approve and sanction a previously unauthorized commitment. *Unauthorized commitment* means an agreement made by a Government representative who lacked the authority to enter into a contract on behalf of the Government.


(b) Procedures are in accordance with the USDA Contracting Desk Book, Part 401.602-3.
