# 750.7108   Contractual requirements.

Every contract modified pursuant to this subpart shall contain:


(a) A citation of the Act and Executive Order 11223;


(b) A brief statement of the circumstances justifying the action; and


(c) With respect to cases falling within section 4 of Executive Order 11223, a statement that the action is necessary to protect the foreign policy interests of the United States.
