# 901.105-1   Publication and code arrangement.

(a) The DEAR and its subsequent changes are published in the _Federal Register,_ cumulative form in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), and Government Printing Office's Electronic CFR at *http://ecfr.gpoaccess.gov.*

(b) The DEAR is issued as chapter 9 of title 48 of the CFR.
