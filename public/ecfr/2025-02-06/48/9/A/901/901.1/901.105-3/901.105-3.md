# 901.105-3   Copies.

Copies of the DEAR published in the _Federal Register_ or Code of Federal Regulations may be purchased from the Superintendent of Documents, Government Printing Office, Washington, DC 20402 or viewed on line at *http://ecfr.gpoaccess.gov* or at *http://management.energy.gov/DEAR.*
