# 903.1004   Contract clauses.

(a) The Contracting Officer shall insert the DOE website address *https://www.energy.gov/sites/prod/files/2017/05/f34/HotlinePoster.pdf* in paragraph (b)(3)(ii) of the clause at FAR 52.203-14, Display of Hotline Poster(s).


(b) The Contracting Officer shall insert the clause at 952.203-1, Identification of Contractor Employees, in all solicitations and contracts for services over the micro-purchase threshold.
