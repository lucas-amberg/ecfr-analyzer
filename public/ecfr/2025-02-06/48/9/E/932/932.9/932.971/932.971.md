# 932.971   Electronic submission of invoices/vouchers.

In general, Contracting Officers should insert the clause at 952.232-7, Electronic Submission of Invoices/Vouchers, in contracts. However, after consultation with the Office of the Chief Financial Officer, the Contracting Officer may approve alternate methods of submission.
