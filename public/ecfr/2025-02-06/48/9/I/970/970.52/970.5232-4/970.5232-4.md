# 970.5232-4   Obligation of funds.

As prescribed in 970.3270(a)(3), insert the following clause: 


*Alternate I* (DEC 2000). As prescribed in 970.3270(a)(3), paragraph (d) of the clause may be omitted in contracts which, expressly or otherwise, provide a contractual basis for equivalent controls in a separate clause.
