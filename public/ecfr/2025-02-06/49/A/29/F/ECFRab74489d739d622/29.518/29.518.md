# ยง 29.518   What reporting must a Tribe provide?

(a) A Tribe must provide reports mandated by statute associated with the funds included in the funding agreement. In accordance with ยง 29.307, the funding agreement will list these reporting requirements. The Tribe will cooperate with the Department to assist the Department in complying with its statutory reporting requirements. No additional reporting will be required of the Tribe.


(b) Notwithstanding paragraph (a) of this section, if the Tribe includes funds for a discretionary or competitive grant in a funding agreement, the Department and the Tribe will negotiate the appropriate reporting requirements to include in the funding agreement.
