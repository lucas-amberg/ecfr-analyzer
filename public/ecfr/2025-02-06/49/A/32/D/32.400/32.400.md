# § 32.400   What are my responsibilities as a(n) DOT awarding official?

As a(n) DOT awarding official, you must obtain each recipient's agreement, as a condition of the award, to comply with the requirements in— 


(a) Subpart B of this part, if the recipient is not an individual; or 


(b) Subpart C of this part, if the recipient is an individual.
