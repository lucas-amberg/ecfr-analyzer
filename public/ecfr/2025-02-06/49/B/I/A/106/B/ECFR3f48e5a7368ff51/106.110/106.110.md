# ยง 106.110   Appealing a PHMSA Action.

You may appeal the following PHMSA actions:


(a) PHMSA's issuance of a final rule or PHMSA's withdrawal of a notice of proposed rulemaking under the rulemaking procedures in this part. However, you may appeal PHMSA's issuance of a direct final rule only if you previously filed comments to the direct final rule (see ยง 106.40(e)).


(b) Any PHMSA decision on a petition for rulemaking.
