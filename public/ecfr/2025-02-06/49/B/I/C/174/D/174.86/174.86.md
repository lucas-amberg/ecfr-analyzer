# ยง 174.86   Maximum allowable operating speed.

(a) For molten metals and molten glass shipped in packagings other than those prescribed in ยง 173.247 of this subchapter, the maximum allowable operating speed may not exceed 24 km/hour (15 mph) for shipments by rail.


(b) For trains transporting any loaded, placarded tank cars containing a material poisonous by inhalation, the maximum allowable operating speed may not exceed 80.5 km/hour (50 mph) for shipments by rail.
