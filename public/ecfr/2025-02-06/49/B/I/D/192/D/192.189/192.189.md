# ยง 192.189   Vaults: Drainage and waterproofing.

(a) Each vault must be designed so as to minimize the entrance of water. 


(b) A vault containing gas piping may not be connected by means of a drain connection to any other underground structure. 


(c) Electrical equipment in vaults must conform to the applicable requirements of Class 1, Group D, of the National Electrical Code, NFPA-70 (incorporated by reference, *see* ยง 192.7).
