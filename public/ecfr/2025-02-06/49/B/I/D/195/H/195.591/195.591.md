# ยง 195.591   In-Line inspection of pipelines.

When conducting in-line inspection of pipelines required by this part, each operator must comply with the requirements and recommendations of API Std 1163, *Inline Inspection Systems Qualification Standard;* ANSI/ASNT ILI-PQ, *Inline Inspection Personnel Qualification and Certification;* and NACE SP0102-2010, *Inline Inspection of Pipelines* (incorporated by reference, see ยง 195.3). An in-line inspection may also be conducted using tethered or remote control tools provided they generally comply with those sections of NACE SP0102-2010 that are applicable.
