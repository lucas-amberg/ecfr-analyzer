# ยง 235.14   Notice.

The FRA will publish notice of the filing of an application or a request for reconsideration of an application in the _Federal Register_ and a copy of such notice will be available at the U.S. Department of Transportation, Docket Operations (M-30), West Building Ground Floor, Room W12-140, 1200 New Jersey Avenue, SE., Washington, DC 20590, and on the Federal Docket Management System's Web site at *http://www.regulations.gov.*
