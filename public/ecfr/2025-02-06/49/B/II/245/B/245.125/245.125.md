# § 245.125   Certification determinations made by other railroads.

(a) A railroad that is considering certification of a person as a dispatcher may rely on certain determinations made by another railroad concerning that person's certification.


(b) A railroad relying on certification determinations made by another railroad shall still be responsible for determining that:


(1) The prior certification is still valid in accordance with the provisions of §§ 245.201 and 245.307;


(2) The person has received training on the physical characteristics of the new territory in accordance with § 245.119; and


(3) The person has demonstrated the necessary knowledge concerning the railroad's operating rules, territory, dispatch systems and technology in accordance with § 245.121.
