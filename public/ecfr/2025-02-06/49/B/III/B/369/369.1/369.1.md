# ยง 369.1   Annual reports of for-hire, non-exempt motor carriers of property, motor carriers of household goods, and dual property carriers.

(a) *Annual Report Form M.* All class I and class II for-hire, non-exempt motor carriers of property, including household goods and dual property motor carriers, must file Motor Carrier Annual Report Form M (Form M). Carriers must file the annual report on or before March 31 of the year following the year to which it relates. For classification criteria, see ยง 369.2.


(b) *Where to file report.* Carriers must file the annual report with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration at the address in ยง 369.6. Blank copies of the report form are available at the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration website *https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/mission/form-m.*
