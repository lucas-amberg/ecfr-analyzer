# ยง 373.101   For-hire, non-exempt motor carrier bills of lading.

Every motor carrier subject to ยง 373.100 shall issue a receipt or bill of lading for property tendered for transportation in interstate or foreign commerce containing the following information:


(a) Names of consignor and consignee.


(b) Origin and destination points.


(c) Number of packages.


(d) Description of freight.


(e) Weight, volume, or measurement of freight (if applicable to the rating of the freight).
