# ยง 1108.8   Relief.

(a) *Relief available.* An arbitrator may grant relief in the form of monetary damages or a rate prescription in rate disputes to the extent they are available under this subpart or as agreed to in writing by the parties. A rate prescription shall not exceed 5 years.


(b) *Relief not available.* No injunctive relief shall be available in Board arbitration proceedings.
