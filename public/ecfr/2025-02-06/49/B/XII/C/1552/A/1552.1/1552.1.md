# ยง 1552.1   Scope.

This part includes requirements for the following persons:


(a) Persons who provide flight training or flight training equipment governed by 49 U.S.C. subtitle VII, part A, to any individual.


(b) Persons who lease flight training equipment.


(c) Non-U.S. citizens who apply for or participate in flight training.


(d) U.S. citizens and U.S. nationals who participate in flight training.
