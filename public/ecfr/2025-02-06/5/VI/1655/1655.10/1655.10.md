# ยง 1655.10   Loan request process.

(a) Any participant may apply for a loan by submitting a completed TSP loan request in the form and manner prescribed by the TSP record keeper.


(b) If a participant has a uniformed services account and a civilian account, a separate loan request must be made for each account.
