# ยง 19.21   Limitation on Federal permits.

No Federal permits will be issued that authorize any person to hunt, shoot, or harass from an aircraft any wildlife, except for migratory birds according to Federal permits issued under part 21 or part 22 of this subchapter when the purpose of the action is consistent with the purpose of the permit regulation.
