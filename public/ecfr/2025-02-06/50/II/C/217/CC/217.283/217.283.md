# § 217.283   Prohibitions.

Except for the takings described in § 217.282 and authorized by an LOA issued under § 217.286 or § 217.287, it is unlawful for any person to do any of the following in connection with the activities described in this subpart:


(a) Violate, or fail to comply with, the terms, conditions, and requirements of this subpart or an LOA issued under § 217.286 or § 217.287;


(b) Take any marine mammal not specified in § 217.282(d);


(c) Take any marine mammal specified in the LOA in any manner other than as specified in the LOA; or


(d) Take any marine mammal specified in § 217.282(d), after NMFS Office of Protected Resources determines such taking results in more than a negligible impact on the species or stocks of such marine mammals.
