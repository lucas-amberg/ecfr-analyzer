# § 218.73   Prohibitions.

Except for incidental takings contemplated in § 218.72(a) and authorized by LOAs issued under §§ 216.106 of this chapter and 218.76, it is unlawful for any person to do any of the following in connection with the activities listed in § 218.70(c):


(a) Violate, or fail to comply with, the terms, conditions, and requirements of this subpart or an LOA issued under §§ 216.106 of this chapter and 218.76;


(b) Take any marine mammal not specified in § 218.72(b);


(c) Take any marine mammal specified in § 218.72(b) in any manner other than as specified in the LOAs; or


(d) Take a marine mammal specified in § 218.72(b) after NMFS determines such taking results in more than a negligible impact on the species or stocks of such marine mammal.
