# ยง 260.22   Appeal inspector selection protocol.

(a) An inspector who did not perform the original product inspection shall be assigned to perform the appeal service; provided that the inspector who made the original product inspection on which an appeal is requested may be authorized to draw the samples when another inspector or licensed sampler is not available in the area where the product is located.


(b) Whenever practical, the appeal service shall be conducted jointly by two inspectors.
