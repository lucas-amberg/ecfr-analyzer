# ยง 260.24   Inspectors.

(a) Federal Government employees licensed or authorized as inspectors will perform inspections.


(b) In addition, qualified persons may be employed and licensed (Cross-Licensee) by the Agency to perform specified inspection services under a joint Federal-State inspection service arrangement.


(c) An Inspector or Cross-Licensee shall perform their duties pursuant to the regulations in this part as directed by the Director.
