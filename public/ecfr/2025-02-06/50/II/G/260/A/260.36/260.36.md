# ยง 260.36   Revocation of approval to use inspection marks and statements.

(a) Approval for use of SIP inspection marks, statements, and insignia will be rescinded when evidence indicates that processing conditions and/or product lots do not meet applicable regulatory, inspection and/or quality requirements per the SIP Manual.


(b) Any affected lot(s) shall be retained and may not enter commerce unless the lot meets minimum regulatory requirements to enter commerce and the SIP insignia is removed.


(c) The establishment or processor shall obtain written clearance from the Agency for the release of product lots that have been put on hold under this part.
