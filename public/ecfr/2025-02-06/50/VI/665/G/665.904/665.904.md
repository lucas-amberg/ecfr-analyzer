# ยง 665.904   Regulated activities.

(a) Commercial fishing is prohibited in the Islands Unit.


(b) Non-commercial fishing is prohibited in the Islands Unit, except as authorized under permit and pursuant to the procedures and criteria established in ยง 665.905.


(c) Commercial fishing outside the Islands Unit and non-commercial fishing within the Islands Unit during the same trip is prohibited.
