# ยง 51.2831   U.S. Export No. 1.

*U.S. Export No. 1* consists of onions which meet the following requirements:


(a) Basic requirements:


(1) Similar varietal characteristics, except color when designated as a specialty or mixed pack;


(2) Mature;


(3) Dormant;


(4) Fairly firm; and,


(5) Fairly well shaped.


(b) Free from:


(1) Decay;


(2) Wet sunscald;


(3) Doubles;


(4) Bottlenecks; and,


(5) Scallions.


(c) Free from damage caused by:


(1) Seedstems;


(2) Splits;


(3) Tops;


(4) Roots;


(5) Dry sunken areas;


(6) Sunburn;


(7) Sprouts;


(8) Freezing;


(9) Peeling;


(10) Cracked fleshy scales;


(11) Watery scales;


(12) Dirt or staining;


(13) Foreign matter;


(14) Mechanical;


(15) Translucent scales;


(16) Disease;


(17) Insects; and,


(18) Other means.


(d) Unless otherwise specified onions are packed in accordance with Export Packing Requirements set forth in ยง 51.2840. (See ยง 51.2837.)
