# § 51.2953   Variety or type specifications.

The variety or type of any lot, of walnuts in the shell may be specified in accordance with the facts as follows:


(a) If the lot is of one named variety, that variety name may be specified, *Provided,* That not over 10 percent, by count, of the walnuts in the lot are of another variety or type than that specified; and,


(b) If the lot is a mixture of two or more distinct varieties or types it may be specified as “Mixed Varieties”.
