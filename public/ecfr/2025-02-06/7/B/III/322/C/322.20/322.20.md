# ยง 322.20   Ports of entry.

A restricted organism may be imported only at a port of entry staffed by an APHIS inspector. 
5 After a restricted organism has been cleared for importation at the port of entry, the organism can only be transported by a bonded commercial carrier immediately and directly from the port of entry to the containment facility or apiary identified on the permit. You may open the package containing the restricted organism only within the containment facility or apiary identified on the permit.
