# § 927.237   Processed pear assessment rate.

On and after July 1, 2023, the following base rates of assessment for pears for processing are established for the Processed Pear Committee:


(a) $7.50 per ton for any or all varieties or subvarieties of pears for canning classified as “summer/fall” excluding pears for other methods of processing;


(b) $0.00 per ton for any or all varieties or subvarieties of pears for processing classified as “winter”; and


(c) $0.00 per ton for any or all varieties or subvarieties of pears for processing classified as “other”.
