# ยง 982.71   Records.

Each handler shall maintain such records of hazelnuts received, held, and disposed of by the handler, and such records detailing such handler's promotion and advertising activities, as may be prescribed by the Board in order to perform its function under this part. Such records shall be retained and be available for examination by authorized representatives of the Board or the Secretary for a period of two years after the end of the marketing year in which the transactions occurred.
