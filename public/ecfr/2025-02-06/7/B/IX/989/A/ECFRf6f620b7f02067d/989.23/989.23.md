# ยง 989.23   File.

*File* means transmit or deliver to the Secretary or Committee, as the case may be, and such act shall be deemed to have been accomplished at the time: 


(a) Of actual receipt by the Secretary or Committee in the event of personal delivery; 


(b) Of receipt at the office of the telegraph company, in case submission is by telegram; or 


(c) Shown by the postmark, in case submission is by mail.
