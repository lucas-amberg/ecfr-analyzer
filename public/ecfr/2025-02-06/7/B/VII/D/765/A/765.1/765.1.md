# ยง 765.1   Introduction.

(a) *Purpose.* This part describes the policies for servicing direct FLP loans, except for borrowers who are delinquent, financially distressed, or otherwise in default on their loan.


(b) *Servicing actions.* Servicing actions described in this part include:


(1) Limited resource reviews;


(2) Graduation to commercial credit;


(3) Application of payments;


(4) Maintaining and disposing of security;


(5) Transfer of security and assumption of debt; and


(6) Servicing accounts of deceased borrowers.


(c) *Loans covered.* The Agency services direct FLP loans under the policies contained in this part. This part is not applicable to Non-program loans, except where noted.
