# § 1005.32   Other reports.

(a) On or before the 20th day after the end of each month, each handler described in § 1000.9(a) and (c) of this chapter shall report to the market administrator any adjustments to distributing plant delivery credit requests as reported pursuant to § 1005.30(a)(5) and (6), and any adjustments to transportation credit requests as reported pursuant to § 1005.30(a)(7) through (9).






(b) In addition to the reports required pursuant to §§ 1005.30, 1005.31, and 1005.32(a), each handler shall report any information the market administrator deems necessary to verify or establish each handler's obligation under the order.
