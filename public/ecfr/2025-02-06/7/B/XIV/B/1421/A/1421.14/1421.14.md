# ยง 1421.14   Obtaining peanut MALs.

(a) Peanuts MALs to individual producers may be obtained through: 


(1) County offices; or 


(2) A designated Marketing Association or a CMA approved by CCC. 


(b) The MAL documents will not be presented for disbursement unless the peanuts pledged as collateral for the MAL are eligible as specified in ยง 1421.8. If the peanuts were ineligible at the time of the disbursement, the total amount disbursed under MAL, or as an LDP, plus charges and interest will be refunded promptly.
