# § 4284.1031   Application requirements.

All applications must include the following items:


(a) *Form SF-424, “Application for Federal Assistance.”* This form must be filled out completely and signed by an authorized representative of the Applicant organization.


(b) *Form RD 4284-1, “Application for the Agriculture Innovation Center Program.”* This form must be filled out completely. Failure to complete any section on the form will result in the application being determined ineligible for funding.
