# ยง 1738.104   Technical assistance.

Projects which will serve communities that meet, at least, three of the priorities as identified in ยง 1738.105(a)(3)(i) may request technical assistance and training from the Agency to:


(a) Prepare reports and surveys necessary to request grants, loans, and loan guarantees for broadband deployment;


(b) Improve management, including financial management, relating to the proposed broadband deployment;


(c) Prepare applications for grants, loans, and loan guarantees; and


(d) Assist with other areas of need as identified by the Agency through a notice in the _Federal Register_.
