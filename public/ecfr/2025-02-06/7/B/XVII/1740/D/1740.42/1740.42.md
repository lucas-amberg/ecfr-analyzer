# ยง 1740.42   Interest rates.

Interest rates for the different funding options that will become available will be included in the _Federal Register_ as part of the funding announcement opening a funding window.


(a) Direct cost-of-money loans shall bear interest at a rate equal to the cost of borrowing to the Department of Treasury for obligations of comparable maturity.


(b) The agency may offer 100 percent loans at a reduced interest rate, and in such cases, the applicable interest rate will be stated in the _Federal Register_ or applicable funding opportunity notice.
