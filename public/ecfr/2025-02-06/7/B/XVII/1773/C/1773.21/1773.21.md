(a) The auditee's governance board should note and record receipt of the reporting package and any action taken in response to the reporting package in the minutes of the board meeting at which such reporting package is presented.


(b) The auditee must furnish RUS with an electronic copy of the reporting package, as described in paragraph (e) of this section, within 120 days of the audit date as provided for in ยง 1773.3.


(c) The auditee must furnish RUS with a copy of its plan for corrective action, if any, within 180 days of the audit date.


(d) The auditee must include a complete reporting package as defined in ยง 1773.2.




(e) All required submissions to RUS described in paragraphs (b) through (d) of this section should be furnished electronically. The electronic copy must be provided in an unlocked or unencrypted Portable Document Format (PDF). All RUS electric and telecommunications auditees shall upload the reporting package to the Agency designated system.
