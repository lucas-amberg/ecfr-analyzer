# ยง 1970.156   Record of Decision.

(a) The ROD is a concise public record of the Agency's decision. The required information and format of the ROD will be consistent with 40 CFR 1505.2.


(b) Once a ROD has been executed by the Agency, the Agency will issue a _Federal Register_ notice indicating its availability to the public.


(c) The ROD may be signed no sooner than 30 days after the publication of EPA's Notice of Availability of the final EIS in the _Federal Register_.
